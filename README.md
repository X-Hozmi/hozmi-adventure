
The year is 1347.  A chill wind whipped across the muddy track as Sir Kaelan and his squire, Thomas, urged their weary horses onward.  Their quest: to reach the fabled city of Zerzura, rumored to hold a cure for the plague ravaging their homeland.  Each day brought new perils – bandits lurking in shadowed forests, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation.  Yet, the whispered promise of Zerzura, a city of shimmering spires and untold riches, fueled their unwavering determination, pushing them ever deeper into the unknown.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Starseeker* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to deliver vital supplies to the struggling colony on Kepler-186f, a planet light-years from Earth.  The journey was fraught with danger; navigating asteroid fields, evading space pirates, and facing the ever-present threat of system malfunctions tested her skills and courage.  But the sight of Kepler-186f, a jewel of emerald and sapphire against the backdrop of the cosmos, was a reward in itself, a testament to humanity's relentless pursuit of exploration amidst the vast, unforgiving expanse of space.
