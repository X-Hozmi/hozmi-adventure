
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his pack heavy with meager supplies, urged his weary horse onward.  He was bound for Constantinople, a journey fraught with peril.  Bandits lurked in the shadowed forests, disease stalked the crowded towns, and the very weather itself was an unpredictable enemy. Yet, the lure of the exotic East, the whispered tales of unimaginable riches and powerful magic, fueled his relentless trek across the treacherous landscapes of Europe and Asia Minor. Each sunrise brought the promise of new challenges, new encounters, and the thrilling uncertainty of the unknown.


Centuries later, Captain Elara Reyes piloted the 'Stardust Drifter' through the shimmering nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to chart the unexplored sectors of the Andromeda galaxy, a journey spanning light years and unimaginable cosmic wonders.  Navigating asteroid fields, evading pirate scavengers in their heavily armed freighters, and deciphering the cryptic signals of alien civilizations were all in a day's work.  The vastness of space, both beautiful and terrifying, held an allure that echoed the medieval adventurer’s yearning for the unknown, a timeless human desire to push boundaries and discover what lies beyond the horizon – or in this case, beyond the furthest reaches of known space.
