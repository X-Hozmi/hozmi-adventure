
The year is 1347.  Dust swirled around Sir Gareth's worn boots as he urged his weary steed onward.  The parchment clutched in his gauntlet promised untold riches – a lost monastery nestled deep within the Whisperwind Mountains, said to hold relics of unimaginable power.  Each creak of the saddle, each rustle in the undergrowth, held the promise of both breathtaking beauty and deadly peril.  Bandits, treacherous passes, and the gnawing hunger of the wilderness were constant companions on his arduous journey across the unforgiving landscape of medieval Europe.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the 'Starseeker' through the iridescent nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to retrieve vital data from a derelict research station orbiting a newly discovered gas giant, a journey spanning light-years and fraught with the unpredictable hazards of space.  Navigating asteroid fields, evading pirate scavengers, and battling the ever-present threat of system failures were all in a day's work for this intrepid explorer, traversing the vast and wondrous expanse of the galaxy.
