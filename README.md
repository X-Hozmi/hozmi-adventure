
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onward.  Ahead lay the rumored lost city of Eldoria, whispered to hold unimaginable riches and ancient magic.  Each creaking wagon wheel, each flash of steel from a passing knight's escort, was a testament to the perils of the journey.  Bandits, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation all loomed, but the lure of Eldoria, a shimmering beacon in the fog of his dreams, kept Thomas pushing forward, one weary step at a time.


Centuries hence, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a potentially habitable exoplanet light-years from Earth. The hum of the hyperdrive was a constant companion, a comforting counterpoint to the vast, unknowable expanse of space.  Navigating asteroid fields, evading pirate scavengers, and coping with the psychological toll of interstellar travel were all commonplace challenges, but the possibility of discovering new life, new worlds, fuelled her intrepid spirit, propelling her ever onward across the unimaginable distances of the cosmos.
