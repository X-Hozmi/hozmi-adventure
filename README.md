
The year is 1347.  Sir Reginald, his surcoat stained with mud and sweat, urged his weary mount onward.  The rough track, barely more than a goat trail, wound through the unforgiving Ardennes forest.  Ahead lay the rumored lost city of Eldoria, a place whispered about in hushed tones by travelling merchants – a city said to hold unimaginable riches and ancient secrets.  Each creak of his saddle, each rustle in the undergrowth, held the potential for peril – bandits, wild beasts, or even worse, the wrath of the ancient spirits said to haunt the forest's depths.  His quest was perilous, his reward uncertain, but the lure of Eldoria burned bright in his heart.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova gripped the controls of the *Stardust Drifter*, her knuckles white.  The jump to the Xylos system was complete, but the swirling nebula ahead promised further challenges.  Navigating the treacherous asteroid field was a delicate dance between speed and precision, a test of both skill and nerve.  Her mission: to retrieve a vital energy source from the planet Xylos IV, a world rumored to be teeming with both valuable resources and hostile, technologically advanced lifeforms. The galaxy stretched before her, a canvas of infinite possibility and unimaginable danger, each star a potential destination, each jump a leap into the unknown.
