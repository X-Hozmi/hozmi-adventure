Journeys were measured in weeks or months, traversing regions largely uncharted and untamed. Dense, whispering forests concealed not just ancient secrets but also the lurking danger of wild beasts and ruthless bandits, while treacherous mountain passes and roaring rivers demanded courage and cunning. Every sunrise brought the promise of a new experience, every sunset the challenge of survival, fostering a spirit of resilience and wonder as they navigated a world both immense and intimately tactile, where legend intertwined with the very soil beneath their boots.
