
The creaking of the wagon wheels was a constant companion as Elara’s caravan snaked its way across the sun-baked plains.  Dust devils danced on the horizon, mirroring the turmoil in her heart.  Each sunrise brought the promise of new dangers – bandits lurking in shadowed forests, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation.  Yet, with every league traversed, the allure of the fabled city of Zerzura, whispered about in hushed tones around crackling campfires, spurred her onward.  The treasures it held, both material and spiritual, fueled her unwavering resolve.


Centuries later, Captain Rexx slammed the hyperdrive into overdrive, the stars blurring into streaks of incandescent light. His starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, leaped across the vast chasm separating the Andromeda galaxy from the Milky Way, a journey undertaken not for gold or glory, but for the survival of his people.  He carried a seed-bank, a precious cargo containing the genetic legacy of a dying world, hoping to find a new home amidst the uncharted nebulae.  The risk was immense, the solitude profound, but the future of his kind rested on his weathered shoulders and the battered hull of his ship.
