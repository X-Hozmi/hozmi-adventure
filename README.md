
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onward.  He clutched the crumpled map, its edges frayed from weeks of relentless travel across the unforgiving terrain.  His quest: to reach the legendary city of Eldoria, whispered to hold the cure for his ailing sister.  Each sunrise brought the threat of bandits, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present gnawing hunger, yet the hope of salvation spurred him on, a flickering candle against the vast, dark landscape of his journey.


Centuries later, Captain Elara Reyes piloted the 'Stardust Drifter' through the shimmering nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to locate the lost colony of Kepler-186f, swallowed by a wormhole decades ago.  Navigating asteroid fields and dodging pirate vessels became routine amidst the breathtaking cosmic vistas.  The loneliness of interstellar travel pressed in, a stark contrast to the vibrant alien worlds she fleetingly glimpsed, each holding the promise of new discoveries, new dangers, and perhaps, the answers she desperately sought.
