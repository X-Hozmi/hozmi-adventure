
The year is 1347.  Dust billows behind Sir Gareth's weary steed as he crests the hill, the sun beating down on his chainmail.  Ahead stretches the unforgiving landscape of the Breton countryside, a tapestry woven with treacherous bogs, dense forests harboring outlaws, and the ever-present threat of bandits preying on lone travelers.  His quest – to deliver a vital message to the besieged castle of Montfort – is a perilous journey, each mile fraught with uncertainty and the ever-present shadow of death.  The clash of steel and the desperate cries of men are not uncommon sounds in this era of constant conflict.

Conversely,  Captain Elara Jax navigated the shimmering nebula of Xylos in her starship, *Stardust Drifter*.  Galaxies spiraled around her, a breathtaking spectacle yet a constant reminder of the vast distances between worlds.  Her mission: to locate the mythical Xylossian crystals, rumored to hold the key to faster-than-light travel.  Though lacking the immediate physical danger of medieval travel, her journey was equally fraught – with unpredictable wormholes, hostile alien encounters, and the crushing weight of isolation amidst the unfathomable expanse of space.
