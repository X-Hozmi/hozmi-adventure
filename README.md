
The year is 1347.  Sir Reginald, his worn leather jerkin creaking with every stride, spurred his destrier onward.  The trail, barely more than a rutted track through the unforgiving Ardennes forest, offered little respite from the relentless sun.  Each bend held the potential for ambush – bandits, wild beasts, or the chilling uncertainty of the unknown.  His quest: to deliver a sacred relic to the besieged city of Avignon, a journey fraught with peril, yet fueled by a knight's unwavering loyalty and a thirst for adventure that dwarfed the physical hardship.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova navigated the shimmering nebula, her starship, the *Starseeker*, a tiny speck against the breathtaking grandeur of the cosmos.  The jump to Xylos, a planet rumored to hold the key to faster-than-light travel, was risky – a treacherous wormhole fraught with unpredictable gravitational forces. But the potential rewards outweighed the danger.  The galaxy stretched before them, a vast expanse of wonders and perils, beckoning her and her crew towards a future forged in the crucible of interstellar exploration.
