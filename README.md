
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onward.  The scent of woodsmoke and damp earth clung to the air, a stark contrast to the rumors of spices and silks whispered in taverns back in London.  He carried a worn map, its edges frayed, promising riches and adventure in the distant lands of the East.  Each creak of the saddle, each rustle in the undergrowth, held the potential for both peril and untold reward—bandits, starvation, or the fabled cities of the Orient awaited him on this arduous journey.


Centuries later, Captain Elara Reyes piloted the starship *Stardust*, a tiny speck against the breathtaking panorama of the Andromeda Galaxy.  Her mission: to locate the lost colony of Xylos, a whisper of a legend among the starfaring clans.  Navigation relied on cryptic stellar charts and the intuition of her seasoned crew, facing down the dangers of nebulae, asteroid fields, and the ever-present threat of pirate raiders.  The journey promised not just discovery, but the potential for first contact with a species beyond human comprehension.
