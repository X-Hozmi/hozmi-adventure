
The year is 1347.  Sir Reginald, his worn leather jerkin creaking with every movement, spurred his destrier onward.  Dust swirled behind them, a brown tide in the wake of their small company.  Their journey across the blighted lands to the rumored city of Eldoria was fraught with peril – bandits lurked in the shadowed forests, starvation gnawed at their heels, and the whispers of monstrous creatures in the untamed wilds kept them constantly vigilant. Each sunrise brought a fresh wave of uncertainty, each nightfall the chilling knowledge that their survival hung precariously in the balance.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the 'Stardust Drifter' through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a potential human colony world light-years from Earth. The vastness of space, a breathtaking panorama of celestial wonders, was both awe-inspiring and terrifying.  Navigation through asteroid fields, the constant threat of solar flares, and the ever-present possibility of encountering unknown alien lifeforms made every hyperspace jump a gamble. But the promise of discovery, the potential to forge a new future among the stars, fueled her unwavering determination.
