No longer bound by continents or oceans, humanity's quest for the unknown now propels starships through hyperspace lanes, bridging the immense distances between solar systems and even galaxies. This new breed of explorer, piloting advanced vessels capable of bending space and time, grapples with challenges far grander than any historical quest. They navigate asteroid fields that dwarf mountain ranges, establish diplomatic ties with enigmatic alien civilizations, and seek out resources on worlds scorched by binary suns or frozen in eternal twilight.
