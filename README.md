
The year is 1347.  Dust coated Sir Kaelan's surcoat as his weary horse plodded through the unforgiving French countryside.  Each creak of the saddle leather echoed the gnawing anxiety in his gut – the King's ransom, entrusted to his care, was heavy, not just in weight, but in the responsibility it represented.  Bandits lurked in every shadowed wood, and the whispers of the Black Death followed close behind the stench of the unwashed masses.  His journey to Avignon, fraught with peril and uncertainty, was a desperate gamble on the future of his kingdom.


Thousands of years hence, Captain Elara Jax navigated the swirling nebula of Xylos, her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, a tiny speck against the cosmic canvas.  The jump to the Andromeda galaxy had been taxing, pushing the vessel's hyperdrive to its limits.  Her mission: to retrieve a rare element vital to sustaining life on her dying home planet.  The vastness of space, while breathtaking, held equally breathtaking dangers – rogue asteroids, temporal anomalies, and the ever-present threat of unknown sentient life forms made this interstellar voyage the ultimate test of courage and skill.
