
The year is 1347.  Dust coated Elara's chausses as she urged her weary horse onward, the setting sun painting the rolling hills of Provence in fiery hues.  Her quest, a perilous journey to deliver a sacred relic to a besieged monastery in the Pyrenees, had already tested her resilience.  Bandits, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of disease stalked her every step, each sunrise a gamble against the unpredictable wilderness and the uncertain loyalty of those she encountered along the way.  Her only companions were her unwavering faith and a worn map, promising salvation, or perhaps, oblivion.

Conversely, Captain Rexx "Comet" Valerian piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos, the ship's engines humming a low, reassuring thrum against the deafening silence of interstellar space. His mission: to retrieve a vital component for the hyperdrive from a remote mining colony on the planet Kepler-186f, a world shrouded in perpetual twilight.  Navigating asteroid fields, evading space pirates, and enduring the psychological strain of months-long journeys across the vast expanse of the cosmos were all part of a galaxy-spanning career –  a life where the unknown promised both unimaginable riches and certain death.
