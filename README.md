
The creak of the wagon wheels was a constant companion, a rhythmic counterpoint to the wind whipping across the vast plains.  Elias, his face weathered by sun and dust, guided his oxen westward, towards the legendary Sunken City rumored to hold unimaginable riches. Bandits, treacherous mountain passes, and dwindling supplies were but minor inconveniences in his quest; the lure of untold treasures and the thrill of the unknown fueled his every step, a true medieval adventurer's spirit burning bright.

Centuries later, Captain Ryla’s starship, the *Stardust Wanderer*, pierced the inky blackness of interstellar space.  Her destination: Xylos, a planet whispered to hold the key to faster-than-light travel.  Navigating asteroid fields, evading space pirates, and battling malfunctioning hyperdrives were commonplace hazards; but the potential for galactic-spanning exploration, and the promise of unlocking the universe's secrets, far outweighed the risks.  Ryla, a modern-day adventurer, pushed onward, her sights set on the cosmos.
