
The year is 1347.  Dust choked the air as Sir Kaelan's band of knights crested the hill, their battered armor gleaming faintly in the afternoon sun.  Before them stretched the vast, untamed wilderness separating them from the rumored city of Eldoria, a place whispered to hold both unimaginable riches and perilous dangers.  Weeks of travel lay ahead, fraught with the threat of bandits, treacherous terrain, and the ever-present gnawing hunger. Each sunrise brought a new challenge, a new test of their courage and resolve in their quest for glory and fortune.


Millennia later, Captain Eva Rostova steered the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula, her breath catching in her throat at the kaleidoscopic beauty.  The journey between the Andromeda Galaxy and the Milky Way had already consumed a generation of her crew, but the promise of a new home, a planet teeming with untold resources, fueled their relentless push through the void.  Warp drives hummed, gravitational currents tugged, and the ever-present threat of pirates and cosmic anomalies kept every crew member on high alert, making this interstellar voyage a constant battle against the vast, unknowable expanse of space.
