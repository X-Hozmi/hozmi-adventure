
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onward.  The pilgrimage to Santiago de Compostela was proving far more arduous than the weathered maps had suggested.  Bandits lurked in the shadowed forests, rivers swelled with unpredictable fury, and the ever-present threat of disease hung heavy in the air. Yet, with each sunrise, a renewed sense of purpose fueled his journey, the whispered tales of miraculous cures and divine grace a beacon in the uncertain landscape.  He carried with him not only his meager possessions, but the hopes and prayers of his village.


Centuries later,  Captain Elara Vance navigated her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  The vibrant colours, a kaleidoscope of cosmic dust and stellar winds, masked the treacherous gravitational currents.  Her mission: to locate the legendary Xylos Crystal, a source of unimaginable power.   Ahead lay uncharted territories, alien landscapes teeming with unknown flora and fauna, and the ever-present risk of encountering hostile civilizations.  But Elara, a seasoned explorer, felt the thrill of the unknown surge within her, a familiar companion on this grand intergalactic quest.
