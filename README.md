
The year is 1347.  Dust coated Sir Kaelan's worn leather jerkin as he urged his weary horse onward, the setting sun painting the craggy peaks of the Dragon's Tooth mountains in fiery hues.  His quest, to reach the mythical city of Eldoria and retrieve the Sunstone, had led him through bandit-infested forests, across treacherous mountain passes, and into the heart of a simmering political conflict.  Each day brought new challenges – starvation, exhaustion, and the constant threat of violence – but the allure of Eldoria, and the whispered legends surrounding the Sunstone, kept his spirit alight.


Centuries hence, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the starship *Stardust* across the inky blackness of the Andromeda galaxy.  Her mission: to locate the lost colony of Xylos, rumored to possess advanced technology capable of saving her dying homeworld.  Navigating asteroid fields, evading the predatory K'tharr warships, and battling the crushing loneliness of interstellar travel were all part of the routine.  Yet, the vast expanse of space, teeming with wonders both beautiful and terrifying, held a thrilling magnetism, fueling Eva's relentless pursuit of Xylos and the hope it represented.
