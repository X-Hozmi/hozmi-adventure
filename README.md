Merchants risked their lives and fortunes navigating bandit-infested roads and treacherous mountain passes to bring exotic goods to distant markets, their caravans laden with silk, spice, and stories. Even pilgrims embarked on arduous treks, their faith guiding them through harsh weather and unknown territories, each step a testament to devotion and resilience.
