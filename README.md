
The year is 1347.  Dust choked the air as Elara's worn boots pounded the rutted track, the midday sun beating down on her and her pack mule.  Ahead, the jagged peaks of the Dragon's Teeth mountains loomed, a formidable barrier between her and the fabled city of Eldoria.  Bandits, starvation, and the ever-present threat of wild beasts were constant companions on this arduous journey, each sunrise bringing a new peril and a renewed sense of determination.  The whispered tales of Eldoria's riches and its lost magic fueled her onward, even as her body screamed for rest.

Centuries hence, Captain Rexx slammed his fist on the console, the tremor shaking the bridge of the *Stardust Drifter*.  Their jump drive sputtered, threatening to leave them stranded adrift in the void between the Andromeda and Milky Way galaxies.  The cartographer's calculations had been off, a minor error with catastrophic consequences.  The vast, alien landscapes of Xylos, a planet brimming with untapped resources and ancient secrets, lay just beyond their grasp, a tantalizing prize threatened by the ever-present dangers of space – rogue asteroids, pirate ships, and the terrifying unknowns lurking in the black between stars.  The mission, already fraught with peril, now hung precariously in the balance.
