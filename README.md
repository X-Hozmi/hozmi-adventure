
The year is 1347.  Dust coated Sir Kaelan's worn leather jerkin as he urged his destrier onward, the endless expanse of the French countryside stretching before them.  Each sunrise brought the promise of new challenges – bandits lurking in shadowed forests, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation.  His quest, to reach the legendary city of Ys, fueled his weary limbs, a mythical prize whispered about in taverns and sung by wandering minstrels. The journey was as much a test of endurance as it was a search for fortune.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the starship *Stardust*, her heart a mirror of the vast, inky blackness surrounding them.  The jump to Kepler-186f had been successful, but the true adventure lay in navigating the planet's alien landscapes and deciphering the cryptic signals emanating from its buried ruins.  Each planet was a new enigma, a testament to the infinite possibilities and unknowable dangers of the cosmos, requiring courage, ingenuity, and an unwavering thirst for discovery that mirrored the spirit of those medieval knights of old.
