
The year is 1347.  Dust choked the air as Elias’s worn boots crunched on the parched earth.  His mule, Bess, plodded onward, her breath ragged in the relentless sun.  Ahead lay the rumored city of Zerzura, a shimmering oasis whispered about in hushed tones by desert nomads.  Bandits, scorching heat, and starvation were constant threats, but the lure of untold riches and the whispers of ancient magic spurred Elias onward, each sunrise a gamble against the unforgiving landscape and the ever-present shadow of despair.  He carried with him not only a worn map, but a burning hope for a better life, a life beyond the harsh realities of his village.

Centuries later,  Captain Eva Rostova piloted the 'Stardust Wanderer' through the swirling nebula of Xylos, her heart thrumming with a different kind of anticipation.  The coordinates pointed to a newly discovered planet, rumored to possess a unique energy source capable of revolutionizing interstellar travel.  Navigating treacherous asteroid fields and dodging the unpredictable gravitational pull of rogue planets were routine hazards.  But the true danger lay in the unknown – the potential for hostile alien life and the ever-present threat of system malfunctions in the vast, uncaring expanse of space. Yet, the promise of scientific discovery and the thrill of exploration outweighed any risk, propelling Eva and her crew further into the cosmos.
