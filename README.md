
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the plains as Sir Kaelan, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his destrier onward.  His quest: to reach the fabled city of Zerzura, rumored to hold the cure for a plague ravaging his homeland.  The journey was a tapestry woven with peril – bandits lurking in shadowed forests, treacherous mountain passes choked with snow, and the ever-present threat of starvation and disease. Yet, with each sunrise, the lure of Zerzura, a beacon of hope in a world consumed by despair, spurred him onward.


Thousands of years hence, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to locate a lost colony on the far side of the galaxy, a journey spanning light years and fraught with dangers unseen.  Navigating asteroid fields, dodging pirate vessels, and repairing critical systems in the vacuum of space tested both her skill and courage. But the promise of finding the colonists, of reuniting them with the rest of humanity, fueled her relentless drive across the cosmic ocean.
