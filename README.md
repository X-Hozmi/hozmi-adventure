
Sir Reginald, his worn leather jerkin creaking with every stride, spurred his destrier onward.  The trail, barely more than a goat path, snaked through the unforgiving peaks of the Dragon's Tooth mountains.  His quest: to reach the fabled city of Eldoria, rumored to hold the lost grimoire of Archmage Theron.  Bandits, treacherous terrain, and the ever-present threat of wild beasts were merely inconveniences in his relentless pursuit of knowledge and glory, a journey testing his courage and resilience with every sun-drenched day and starlit night.


Centuries hence, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Wanderer* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a potentially habitable exoplanet light-years from Earth.  Navigating asteroid fields, dodging the gravitational pull of rogue planets, and battling the ever-present threat of space pirates was all in a day's work for the hardened explorer.  The vast emptiness of space held both breathtaking beauty and terrifying unknowns, each jump to hyperspace a gamble against the infinite possibilities of the cosmos.
