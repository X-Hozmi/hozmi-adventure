
Sir Reginald, his armor gleaming faintly in the twilight, spurred his destrier onward.  The rough track, barely a path through the dense Black Forest, offered little comfort.  Each creak of his saddle, each rustle in the undergrowth, heightened the tension of their journey. They carried a vital message to the King, a message that could decide the fate of the kingdom, and every mile felt like a gamble against bandits, wild beasts, and the ever-present shadow of the encroaching night.  The promise of adventure, laced with danger, spurred him on.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a potentially habitable exoplanet light-years from Earth.  The journey was long, fraught with the unpredictable hazards of hyperspace and the ever-present threat of pirate raiders.  Yet, the allure of discovery, of charting unmapped territories amongst the stars, and the hope of finding a new home for humanity fueled her and her crew. The vast, inky blackness held both unimaginable wonders and terrifying unknowns.
