Whether a knight on a quest for glory, a merchant seeking lucrative new trade routes, or a devout pilgrim journeying to a sacred shrine, every step across the rolling hills, through dense, sun-dappled forests, and over treacherous mountain passes was an odyssey. The unknown loomed large, promising both dire peril and the potential for untold discovery, a stark contrast to the predictable safety of home.
