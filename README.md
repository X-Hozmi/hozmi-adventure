
The year is 1347.  Dust coated the roughspun tunic clinging to Elara's back as she urged her weary horse onward.  The midday sun beat down mercilessly on the parched landscape, the only respite the occasional scrubby oak offering a sliver of shade.  Her journey from Canterbury to the Holy Land was a perilous one, fraught with bandit raids, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of disease. Each sunrise brought a fresh wave of uncertainty, but the unwavering faith in her heart, and the promise of Jerusalem, spurred her onward through the unforgiving medieval world.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova navigated the shimmering nebula of Xylos in her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*.  Ahead lay Kepler-186f, a terraformed planet promising untold riches and scientific breakthroughs.  But the journey wasn't without its hazards: rogue asteroids, unstable wormholes, and the ever-present threat of pirate incursions made every jump a gamble.  Yet, the allure of the unknown, the thrill of charting unmapped galaxies, and the potential for discovery fueled her courage, making the vast emptiness of space a thrilling arena for her adventure.
