Leaving the familiar confines of one's village meant venturing into a world brimming with untamed wilderness, treacherous mountain passes, and dense, bandit-infested forests. Every journey, whether for trade, pilgrimage, or the simple desire to see what lay beyond the next horizon, was a test of endurance and wit, demanding a sturdy resolve against harsh weather, unknown diseases, and the ever-present threat of human malice.

Yet, it was precisely this crucible of challenge that forged legendary tales and transformed ordinary folk.
