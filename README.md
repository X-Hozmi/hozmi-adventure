
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onward.  He clutched the worn map, its parchment brittle with age, detailing a perilous route across the treacherous Black Forest to reach the rumored lost city of Eldoria.  Each rustle in the undergrowth sent a shiver down his spine, the tales of forest goblins and ravenous wolves a constant companion.  He yearned for the riches Eldoria promised, but more than that, he craved the thrill of the unknown, the glory of overcoming such a daunting journey.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the 'Stardust Drifter' through the shimmering nebula of Xylos.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a planet whispered to hold the key to interdimensional travel.  The hum of the hyperdrive was a comforting drone against the vast, inky blackness punctuated by distant star clusters.  Navigating treacherous asteroid fields and avoiding the predatory space pirates of the Orion Arm was a daily challenge, but the allure of unlocking the universe's greatest secrets, a feat only dreamed of by generations past, propelled her forward, a modern-day explorer charting the uncharted cosmos.
