
The year is 1347.  Dust choked the air as Sir Reginald spurred his destrier onward, the relentless sun beating down on his battered armor.  His quest: to reach the mythical city of Eldoria, whispered to hold the cure for his King's deadly illness.  Each day was a gamble, fraught with peril: bandits lurking in shadowed forests, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation. Yet, the lure of Eldoria, and the hope it represented, fueled his weary limbs, pushing him ever closer to his uncertain destiny.


Thousands of years hence, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to locate a lost colony scattered across the Andromeda galaxy, a task that demanded navigating treacherous asteroid fields, evading hostile K'tharr warships, and enduring the crushing loneliness of interstellar travel.  The vastness of space held both wonder and terrifying unknowns, but the promise of reuniting lost families – and perhaps uncovering new worlds – spurred her on through the endless black.
