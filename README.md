
The year is 1347.  Dust swirled around Elara's worn boots as she crested the hill, the vast, unforgiving landscape of the French countryside stretching before her.  Her meager pack held only essential supplies – dried meat, a waterskin, and a worn map promising a shortcut to the bustling port city of Marseille.  Bandits, starvation, and the ever-present threat of disease were constant companions on her journey, but the lure of a new life, a life beyond the confines of her tiny village, fueled her weary steps. Each sunrise brought a new challenge, each sunset a hard-won victory, etching the adventure deeply into her soul.


Conversely, centuries later, Captain Rexx navigated the shimmering nebula known as the Serpent's Kiss.  His starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, pulsed with energy as it jumped between galaxies, the vibrant cosmic tapestry a breathtaking sight even after years of such voyages. His mission: to locate the mythical Xylos planet, rumored to hold the key to interstellar peace.  The journey was fraught with peril – unpredictable wormholes, hostile alien encounters, and the constant gnawing uncertainty of the vast unknown. But the allure of discovery, the potential for a galaxy-altering achievement, propelled him onward, a lone explorer in the boundless expanse of space.
