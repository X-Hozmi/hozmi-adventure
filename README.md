
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's worn boots as she and her mule, Bess, crested the hill.  Ahead stretched the endless expanse of the French countryside, a tapestry woven with emerald fields and sapphire rivers, but also shadowed by the looming threat of bandits and the unpredictable whims of nature.  Each sunrise brought the promise of new challenges – navigating treacherous mountain passes, bartering for supplies in unfamiliar villages, and dodging the suspicious glares of local lords.  Her quest for the legendary lost city of Ys was a perilous journey, but the whispered tales of its wonders fueled her courage.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos, its vibrant hues painting her cockpit in an ethereal glow.  Their mission: to chart a new hyperspace route connecting the Andromeda and Milky Way galaxies, a feat considered impossible by most.  Eva, however, thrived on the impossible.  Each jump through hyperspace was a gamble, a breathtaking leap of faith into the unknown, where unpredictable gravitational fields and rogue asteroids posed a constant threat. The vastness of space was both terrifying and exhilarating, a testament to humanity's boundless ambition and insatiable thirst for exploration.
