
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she crested the pass, the vast, unforgiving landscape of the Pyrenees stretching before her.  Each day was a gamble – bandit raids were common, food scarce, and the whispers of the plague ever-present.  Yet, the thrill of the unknown propelled her onward, towards the fabled city of Samarkand, rumored to hold the secret to healing her ailing brother.  The journey was a tapestry woven with hardship and unexpected kindness, encounters with grizzled pilgrims and shrewd merchants, all against the backdrop of towering mountains and sun-drenched plains.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the 'Starseeker' through the swirling nebula of Xylos, her destination a newly discovered planet in the Andromeda galaxy.  Faster-than-light travel was commonplace, but the dangers were no less real.  Space pirates, rogue asteroids, and unpredictable wormholes posed constant threats.  Yet, Eva, driven by a thirst for discovery and the promise of uncharted worlds teeming with life (or death), navigated the cosmos with unwavering determination.  Each jump into hyperspace was a leap of faith, a thrilling plunge into the vast, uncaring beauty of the universe.
