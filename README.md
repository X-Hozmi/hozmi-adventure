
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's horse as she urged it onward, the rhythmic clop of hooves a counterpoint to the wind whipping through her braided hair.  Her journey across the treacherous Ardennes forest was far from the romanticized tales of chivalry;  it was a grueling test of endurance, fraught with the dangers of bandits, mud-choked paths, and the ever-present threat of starvation.  Each sunrise brought a renewed sense of hope, a fragile ember against the vast, unforgiving wilderness, fueling her quest to reach the distant monastery rumored to hold the cure for her ailing brother.


Centuries later, Captain Rexa steered her starship *Stardust* through the swirling nebula of Xylos, its vibrant hues a breathtaking but deceptive beauty.  The journey between galaxies was measured not in days, but in light-years, each jump a calculated risk requiring pinpoint navigation and a healthy dose of faith in hyperdrive technology.  Beyond the breathtaking cosmic vistas lay the promise of a new world, a planet teeming with unknown life and potentially valuable resources – a reward worth the perils of navigating asteroid fields and evading the relentless pursuit of the Kryll Hegemony.
