
The creak of the wagon wheels was a constant companion, the rhythmic beat a counterpoint to the wind whipping across the vast, uncharted plains.  Sir Kaelan, his worn leather jerkin stiff with dried mud, squinted at the setting sun.  Each day brought new perils – bandits lurking in shadowed forests, treacherous river crossings, and the ever-present threat of starvation.  Yet, the lure of the mythical Sunstone, whispered to lie beyond the Dragon's Tooth Mountains, fueled his weary bones and spurred him onward, a solitary knight on a quest that tested both body and spirit.


Centuries hence, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through a nebula painted in swirling hues of amethyst and emerald.  The jump to Kepler-186f had been flawless, but the long-range sensors still hummed with static, searching for signs of habitable zones. Her crew, a diverse band of xeno-linguists and terraformers, monitored their vitals and equipment, their faces illuminated by the ethereal glow of the cosmos.  The vastness of space was a terrifying and exhilarating constant, a reminder of the boundless possibilities – and the equally boundless dangers – of interstellar travel.
