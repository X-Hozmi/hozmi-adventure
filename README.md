
The year is 1347.  Dust swirled around Elara's worn boots as she urged her mule onward, the setting sun painting the craggy peaks of the Pyrenees a fiery orange.  Her journey to the rumored city of Eldoria, whispered to hold ancient secrets and untold riches, was fraught with peril.  Bandit ambushes, treacherous mountain passes, and the gnawing hunger were constant companions, but the allure of the unknown pushed her ever forward, a testament to the enduring human spirit that seeks adventure even in the harshest of landscapes.  Each sunrise brought a new challenge, each sunset a new memory etched onto the map of her life.

Alternatively, Captain Rexx "Razor" Talon piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the shimmering nebula of Xylos.  The jump to the Kepler-186f system had been rough, the hyperspace rift spitting them out near a rogue asteroid field.  Their mission: to retrieve a rare crystalline energy source vital to the Galactic Federation.  Navigating treacherous asteroid fields and evading the ever-present threat of space pirates was routine for Razor and his crew.  The vastness of space held both wonder and danger, and tonight, the odds were stacked against them, but the promise of untold riches – and the thrill of the chase – kept their engines roaring towards the unknown.
