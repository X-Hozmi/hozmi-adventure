
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she crested the hill, the vast, sun-baked expanse of the Iberian Peninsula stretching before her.  Her worn leather satchel held more than just dried meats and a chipped waterskin; it contained the hopes of her family, a desperate journey to find rumored work in a far-off city.  Each creak of the saddle, each rustle in the tall grasses, spoke of the unknown dangers lurking – bandits, starvation, and the ever-present threat of disease. Yet, the allure of a new life, a chance at prosperity, pushed her onward, a tiny silhouette against the immensity of the medieval world.


Conversely, Captain Rexx "Comet" Valerius piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through a nebula, the kaleidoscopic colours a stark contrast to the cold, calculating precision of his navigation.  His mission: to chart the unexplored K'tharr system, a treacherous cluster of planets rumored to hold a rare energy source vital to the Galactic Federation.  The hum of the hyperdrive was a constant companion, a lullaby against the infinite void.  While the dangers were different – asteroid fields, rogue energy storms, and possibly hostile alien life – the spirit of adventure burned just as brightly, a human desire to push boundaries across the vast canvas of the cosmos.
