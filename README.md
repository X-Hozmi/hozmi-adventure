
The year is 1347.  Sir Gareth, his surcoat stained with mud and sweat, urged his weary mount onward.  The journey across the breadth of France had been brutal; bandits lurking in shadowed forests, treacherous mountain passes slick with rain, and the constant gnawing hunger a more persistent foe than any knight.  Yet, the lure of the legendary city of Ys, whispered to hold untold riches and magic, spurred him onwards.  Each sunrise painted the sky with a promise, each sunset brought the chilling reality of the vast, untamed land still before him.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the 'Stardust Drifter' through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  The journey between galaxies was less perilous than the medieval treks, but no less daunting.  The vast distances and unpredictable warp storms demanded constant vigilance, her crew a motley band of specialists who relied on each other as much as their advanced technology.  The potential reward, a planet rumored to hold the key to faster-than-light travel, made the risks worth taking – a new era of exploration waited on the other side of the cosmos.
