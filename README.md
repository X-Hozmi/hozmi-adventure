
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's weary mule as she crested the pass, the vast, unforgiving expanse of the Pyrenees stretching before her.  Her worn leather satchel held little besides dried figs and a chipped map, her only companions the biting wind and the ghosts of travelers past.  Each sunrise brought a fresh test – bandit raids, treacherous mountain paths, the constant gnawing fear of starvation. But the whispered tales of the legendary city of Eldoria, nestled in the heart of unexplored lands, fuelled her onward, a beacon in the harsh reality of medieval travel.  The adventure was perilous, but the potential reward, untold riches and knowledge, far outweighed the risks.


Centuries later, Captain Rexx slammed his fist on the console, the crimson nebula swirling outside the viewport of his starship, *Stardust Drifter*.  Their hyperdrive jump had gone awry, leaving them adrift near the Kraken's Maw, a region notorious for its volatile wormholes and pirate fleets.  Navigation charts were useless in this chaotic corner of the Andromeda galaxy, and the crew, a ragtag bunch of xenobiologists and mercenaries, were growing restless.  But Rexx, a seasoned spacefarer with a thirst for the unknown, saw not a setback, but another thrilling chapter in his intergalactic odyssey.  The treasure wasn't gold or jewels, but the chance to discover a new life form, a new planet, a new mystery to unravel amongst the stars.
