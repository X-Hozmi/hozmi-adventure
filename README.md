
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she traversed the treacherous Dragon's Tooth pass, the wind whipping her cloak around her like a frantic bird.  Ahead lay the rumored city of Zerzura, a shimmering mirage in the endless expanse of the desert.  Bandits, scorching sun, and the ever-present threat of starvation were constant companions on this perilous journey, but the whispered tales of Zerzura's untold riches and ancient secrets fueled her onward.  Each rocky outcrop, each whispering wind, held the promise of adventure, and the fear was strangely exhilarating.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the nebula of Xylos, its iridescent hues a breathtaking backdrop to the vast emptiness of space.  Their mission: to chart a newly discovered wormhole leading to the Andromeda galaxy. The risks were astronomical – rogue asteroids, unpredictable gravitational fields, and the ever-present danger of encountering unknown alien lifeforms. Yet, the potential discovery of new worlds, new technologies, and answers to humanity's deepest questions made the journey, however perilous, undeniably worthwhile.  The cosmos was their canvas, and exploration their brush.
