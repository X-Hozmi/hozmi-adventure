
The year is 1347.  Sir Kaelan, his worn leather jerkin smelling of woodsmoke and sweat, spurred his destrier onward.  The trail, barely more than a rutted track through the dense Ardennes forest, snaked towards the unknown lands beyond.  Rumors of a lost city, shimmering with gold and guarded by mythical creatures, fueled his perilous journey. Each creak of his saddle, each rustle in the undergrowth, held the promise of adventure – or death.  He carried with him not only his sword but the weight of his family's hopes, and the burning thirst for glory that only a medieval adventurer could understand.


Centuries hence, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the starship *Stardust* across the void between galaxies.  The swirling nebulae outside her viewport painted a breathtaking, yet terrifying, landscape.  Her mission: to chart the unmapped Andromeda galaxy, searching for habitable worlds and new resources for a humanity stretched thin across the stars.  The dangers weren't mythical beasts but rogue asteroids, unpredictable wormholes, and the ever-present threat of encountering unknown alien civilizations.  Yet, the vastness of space and the promise of discovery held a magnetic pull, driving her further into the cosmic unknown.
