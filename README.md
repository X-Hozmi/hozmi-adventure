
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's worn boots as she urged her mule onward, the setting sun painting the rugged landscape in hues of blood orange and bruised purple.  Her journey across the treacherous Spine of the World mountains was fraught with peril – bandits lurking in shadowed valleys, treacherous river crossings, and the ever-present threat of starvation. Yet, the whispered legends of the lost city of Eldoria, a place said to hold unimaginable riches and ancient secrets, spurred her onward. Each aching muscle, each biting wind, fueled her determination to reach her destination.

Centuries later, Captain Jax hurled his star freighter, the *Stardust Drifter*, through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  The vibrant colours painted a breathtaking panorama across his cockpit viewport, but the beauty masked the very real dangers of navigating uncharted space.  His mission: to deliver a vital shipment of Xylossian crystals to the galactic capital, a journey fraught with pirate attacks, unpredictable wormholes, and the ever-present threat of engine failure light-years from any known civilization.  The vastness of space, while awe-inspiring, held a cold, indifferent power that tested Jax's courage with every passing star.
