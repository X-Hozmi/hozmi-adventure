
The year is 1347.  Dust billows behind Elara’s mule as she traverses the unforgiving landscape of the Rhône Valley.  Each sunrise brings the threat of bandit raids, dwindling supplies, and the ever-present discomfort of rough terrain.  Her quest – to reach Avignon and deliver a vital message to the Pope – is a gamble against the elements and the shadowy figures that haunt the forgotten roads.  The camaraderie of her fellow travelers, however, offers solace against the loneliness, and the promise of reward fuels her weary steps.  Each day is a battle for survival, a small victory in the larger fight for delivery.

Alternatively,  Captain Jaxx navigated the shimmering nebula of Xylos, his starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, a tiny speck against the colossal canvas of space.  The jump to the Andromeda galaxy had strained the hyperdrive, leaving them adrift with dwindling fuel and a precarious navigation system.  Across the void, whispers of the legendary Xylos crystals – rumored to power entire star systems – beckoned.  Facing down pirate attacks from the Kryll, navigating asteroid fields, and repairing the ship's systems, Jaxx and his crew wagered everything on this perilous voyage. The galaxy itself seemed to conspire against them, but the allure of discovery and the camaraderie of his crew pushed them forward.
