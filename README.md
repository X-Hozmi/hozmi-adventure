
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she traversed the sun-baked plains of Aragon.  Her worn leather jerkin held little besides dried figs and a chipped flint knife, but her heart held a burning ember of purpose – to reach the fabled city of Zaragoza, rumored to hold a cure for her ailing brother.  Each creak of the saddle, each rustle of the wind through the sparse scrub, was a whispered challenge, a reminder of the dangers lurking in the shadows of the unforgiving landscape: bandits, starvation, and the ever-present threat of disease.  Yet, with every league conquered, her spirit soared, fueled by hope and a fierce determination to overcome.


Centuries later, Captain Jax navigated the swirling nebula of Xylos, his starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, a tiny speck against the immeasurable canvas of space.  His mission: to retrieve a vital data crystal from the forgotten mining colony on Kepler-186f, a planet shrouded in perpetual twilight.  Navigating asteroid fields and evading pirate scavengers was commonplace; the true danger lay in the unpredictable temporal anomalies that plagued the Kepler system, threatening to strand him in the past or even obliterate his ship.  But Jax, a veteran of countless galactic expeditions, pressed onward, his focus fixed on the mission's critical importance and the promise of a hefty reward awaiting him on his return to the galactic hub.
