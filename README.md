
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's horse as she urged it onward, the setting sun painting the endless fields of France a fiery orange.  Her quest: to reach the mythical city of Ys, rumored to lie hidden beneath the western sea.  Each day brought new challenges – bandits lurking in shadowed forests, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present gnawing hunger.  But the lure of the unknown, the whispered legends of Ys, fueled her relentless journey, a testament to the human spirit's enduring thirst for adventure.


Centuries later, Captain Ryla Kael steered the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to deliver a vital shipment of rare isotopes to the Kepler-186f colony, a jewel nestled within a far-flung star system.  Navigating treacherous asteroid fields and dodging the ever-present threat of pirate raiders demanded every ounce of her skill and courage.  Yet, the vast cosmic canvas, the breathtaking beauty of unexplored planets, and the promise of galactic trade rewarded the risks, making every light-year traversed a step into the boundless frontier of human exploration.
