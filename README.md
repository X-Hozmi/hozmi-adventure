
The creaking wagon wheels sang a familiar song across the rutted track, each bump a reminder of the vast distance still separating Sir Gareth from his destination.  He’d traded the safety of his lord’s manor for the unpredictable thrill of the King’s Highway, a ribbon of dirt and dust weaving through sun-drenched fields and shadowed forests.  Bandit ambushes, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation were all part of the adventure, but the lure of the fabled city of Eldoria, whispered to hold secrets of unimaginable power, fueled his relentless journey.


Centuries hence, Captain Eva Rostova navigated the shimmering nebula of Xylos, her starship, the *Stardust Wanderer*, a tiny speck against the breathtaking expanse.  Each jump through hyperspace was a gamble, a leap of faith across lightyears of void.  Her mission: to locate the lost colony of Kepler-186f, a whisper of a signal their only clue.  Facing down pirate raiders, navigating treacherous asteroid fields, and the constant hum of loneliness amidst the infinite stars, Eva's journey was a testament to human courage and the boundless thirst for discovery across the cosmos.
