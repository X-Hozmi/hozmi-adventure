
Sir Gareth, his worn leather jerkin creaking with every stride, urged his destrier onward.  The sun beat down on the dusty track, the scent of thyme and sweat thick in the air.  His quest, to reach the fabled city of Eldoria before winter's grip tightened, was a perilous one.  Bandits lurked in the shadowed forests, and the whispering rumors of monstrous beasts in the mountains chilled him to the bone. Each sunrise brought new challenges, from fording icy rivers to navigating treacherous mountain passes, but the lure of Eldoria, and the promise of untold riches and glory, spurred him onward.


Centuries hence, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Starseeker* through the iridescent nebula of Xylos.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a potentially habitable exoplanet light-years from Earth.  The journey was a symphony of calculated risks, navigating asteroid fields, battling solar flares, and battling the creeping loneliness of interstellar space.  But the weight of humanity's future rested on her shoulders, the hope of finding a new home a beacon guiding her through the endless black. The vastness of the cosmos, both terrifying and awe-inspiring, fueled her determination.
