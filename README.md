
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she traversed the sun-baked plains of Anatolia, the scent of thyme and sweat heavy in the air.  Her worn leather satchel held not gold, but a king's desperate plea – a request for a mythical herb said to cure a plague that ravaged his land.  Each creak of the saddle, each rustle in the tall grasses, held the promise of both peril and discovery, for the road to Byzantium was fraught with bandits, treacherous terrain, and the ever-present threat of the encroaching desert.  This journey, though arduous, promised glory—or oblivion.


Centuries later, Captain Jaxx navigated the shimmering nebula of Xylos in her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*.  Ahead lay Kepler-186f, a planet rumored to hold the key to faster-than-light travel, a secret coveted by warring galactic empires.  Hyperspace jumps were risky, wormholes unpredictable, and the ever-present threat of pirate raids made every kilometer a gamble.  But the lure of unlocking the universe’s ultimate secret far outweighed the dangers, fueling Jaxx's relentless pursuit across the cosmos.
