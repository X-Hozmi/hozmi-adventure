
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the desolate plains as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his weary horse onward.  He carried a king's ransom in silks and spices, a perilous cargo purchased in Constantinople and destined for the far-off markets of Novgorod.  Bandits, starvation, and the unpredictable fury of nature were his constant companions on this arduous journey across the fragmented kingdoms of Europe.  Each sunrise brought the hope of reaching his destination, balanced against the ever-present fear of never seeing home again.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the 'Stardust Drifter' through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to locate the legendary Xylos Crystal, a power source capable of bridging galaxies.  Navigating treacherous asteroid fields and evading the aggressive K'tharr pirates, Eva’s journey was a constant battle against the unforgiving vastness of space.  The allure of the crystal, promising unimaginable power and untold riches, pushed her relentlessly forward despite the overwhelming odds.
