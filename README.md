
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's horse as she urged it onward, the setting sun painting the endless plains of France a fiery orange.  Her worn leather satchel held little beyond a few meager coins, a chipped map detailing a rumored hidden monastery, and a fierce determination to reach it before winter's grip tightened.  Bandits, treacherous terrain, and the ever-present threat of illness were daily companions on her quest for a legendary healing herb, a journey that tested both her body and her spirit.  Each sunrise brought a new challenge, a new opportunity to prove her resilience in the face of a medieval world brimming with danger and untold adventure.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the 'Starseeker' through the swirling nebula of Xylos, the crimson dust a stark contrast to the cold, calculating precision of her ship's instruments.  Her mission: to retrieve a vital data core from a derelict research station orbiting a newly discovered planet, a planet whispered to hold the key to faster-than-light travel.  The risks were immense - hostile alien lifeforms, unpredictable gravitational anomalies, and the ever-present threat of system failure.  But the allure of galactic exploration, the promise of unlocking the universe's deepest secrets, fuelled her relentless drive across the vast emptiness between worlds.
