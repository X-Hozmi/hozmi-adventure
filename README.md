
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's weary horse as she navigated the treacherous, rock-strewn path across the Pyrenees.  Her worn leather satchel held not gold, but a single, precious seed – a rumored cure for the plague ravaging her village.  Each sunset brought the threat of bandits or starvation, but the promise of salvation spurred her onward through sun-drenched valleys and windswept mountain passes, a solitary figure against the vast, unforgiving landscape of medieval Europe.  The journey was a tapestry woven with hardship and hope, a testament to human resilience in the face of overwhelming odds.


Millennia later, Captain Rex 'Jax' Valerian piloted the 'Stardust Drifter' through the shimmering nebula of Xylos.  His mission: to deliver a vital shipment of antimatter to the Xylossian colony, a fragile outpost clinging to existence on the edge of explored space.  Navigating asteroid fields and avoiding the predatory 'Void Serpents' was a daily challenge, the immense distances between stars a constant reminder of his isolation.  Yet, the vastness of the cosmos held a thrilling allure, each jump to hyperspace a gamble, each new planet a potential treasure trove of discovery in this unforgiving, yet breathtaking expanse.
