
The year is 1347.  Dust choked the air as Elara's worn leather boots crunched on the parched earth.  Her caravan, a motley collection of merchants, pilgrims, and mercenaries, snaked its way across the unforgiving plains towards the mythical city of Zerzura.  Bandits lurked in the shadows of crumbling ruins, and the whispers of djinn and desert spirits filled the night. Each sunrise brought the promise of new dangers – scorching heat, starvation, or perhaps, the discovery of long-lost treasures hidden beneath the shifting sands. Survival depended on courage, cunning, and a healthy dose of luck.


Centuries later, Captain Rexx slammed his fist on the console, charting a course through the Andromeda Galaxy.  His starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, a nimble vessel capable of warping across vast interstellar distances, was headed for Xylos, a planet rumored to hold the key to unlocking faster-than-light travel.  The journey was fraught with peril: rogue asteroids, unpredictable nebulae, and the ever-present threat of pirate fleets prowling the black.  But the allure of unlocking the universe's secrets, of charting new worlds and discovering unimaginable wonders, far outweighed the risks.
