
**Future Era (Travel Between Planets and Galaxies)**

Forget dusty trails and perilous forests; the ultimate adventure now spans light-years. Humanity has ventured beyond the cradle, propelled by warp drives and stardrives into a cosmos teeming with the unknown. Every jump is a gamble, every nebula a potential graveyard or a gateway to wonders. Explorers chart routes through asteroid fields, evade void beasts capable of swallowing starships whole, and navigate political complexities between burgeoning stellar empires and ancient, enigmatic federations. The silent void itself is an adversary, its vastness a constant reminder of the fragility of life.

Yet, the allure is irresistible.
