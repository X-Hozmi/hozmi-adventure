
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's horse as she rode across the sun-baked plains of Castile, the scent of rosemary and sweat heavy in the air.  Her worn leather satchel held not gold, but a king's desperate plea: a cure for the creeping plague.  Each creak of her saddle, each rustle in the tall grass, held the potential for bandits, starvation, or worse.  Yet, Elara pressed on, her heart a fierce drum against the rhythmic beat of her horse's hooves, driven by a mission that transcended personal peril. This journey, perilous and unpredictable, held the hope of a kingdom.


Centuries later, Captain Jaxx navigated the Xylos Nebula, the swirling colors a hypnotic backdrop to the relentless hum of the *Stardust Drifter*.  His destination: Kepler-186f, a planet whispered to hold the key to interstellar peace.  But the galaxy was a treacherous place, filled with pirates, rogue AI, and gravitational anomalies that could crush a ship like an eggshell.  Jaxx’s crew, a ragtag bunch of specialists bound by shared ambition and a thirst for the unknown, faced each challenge with grit, their courage fueled by the vast, breathtaking beauty and terrifying dangers of the cosmos.
