
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onward.  His quest: to reach the fabled city of Zerzura, whispered to hold the secrets of ancient magic.  Days bled into weeks, each sunrise revealing a new challenge – bandits lurking in shadowed forests, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation.  Yet, the lure of the unknown, the whispered legends of Zerzura, spurred him on, each hardship fueling the fire of his adventure.


Millennia later, Captain Elara Vance piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos. Her mission: to chart a new hyperspace route to the Andromeda galaxy, a journey fraught with the perils of unstable wormholes and unknown celestial phenomena.  The vast, inky blackness of space held a different kind of terror than the medieval wilds, but the thrill of discovery, the potential for encountering alien civilizations, and the sheer audacity of her journey matched, and perhaps surpassed, the legendary quests of old.
