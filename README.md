
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onward.  The scent of woodsmoke and damp earth filled his nostrils, a stark contrast to the tales of spice and silk he’d heard from returning traders.  His quest, to reach the legendary city of Samarkand, felt both daunting and exhilarating; each sunrise brought the promise of new landscapes, perilous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of bandits lurking in the shadows. The weight of his meager possessions felt insignificant compared to the weight of his ambition.


Thousands of years hence, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the 'Starseeker' through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  The vibrant colours painted across the viewport were a breathtaking distraction from the complex navigation required to jump across galaxies.  Her mission, to locate a rumored habitable planet beyond known charted space, was far from certain, with the risk of encountering uncharted cosmic phenomena or hostile alien civilizations a constant companion. Yet, the thrill of exploring the unknown, of pushing the boundaries of human knowledge, far outweighed the danger.
