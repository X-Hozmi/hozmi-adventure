
The year is 1347.  Sir Kaelan, his worn surcoat flapping in the wind, urged his destrier onward.  The rutted track wound through a landscape of brooding forests and treacherous bogs, the destination: the mythical city of Eldoria, rumored to hold a cure for the plague ravaging his homeland.  Each creak of his saddle, each rustle in the undergrowth, held the potential for ambush or worse – a band of outlaws, a starving wolf pack, or the insidious grip of the swamp itself.  His journey was a gamble against the elements and the unknown, a testament to unwavering hope and sheer grit.


Millennia later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to retrieve a vital data core from the derelict research station orbiting Kepler-186f.  Navigating asteroid fields, dodging pirate vessels, and battling the ever-present threat of temporal distortions, she relied on her crew's skill and her own unwavering resolve.  The vastness of space, a canvas of breathtaking beauty and terrifying emptiness, held the promise of unimaginable discovery, but also the cold, uncaring reality of death in the infinite void.
