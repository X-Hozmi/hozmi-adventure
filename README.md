
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his weary horse onward.  His quest, to reach the mythical city of Eldoria rumored to hold a cure for his ailing sister, led him through treacherous mountain passes and across unforgiving plains.  Each sunrise brought new perils – bandits lurking in shadowed forests, raging rivers to ford, and the constant threat of starvation and disease. Yet, the unwavering hope for his sister fueled his relentless journey, transforming the harsh landscape into a tapestry of thrilling challenges and breathtaking vistas.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the starship *Stardust* across the vast gulf between the Andromeda and Milky Way galaxies.  Her mission: to locate a lost colony, a beacon of hope against the encroaching darkness of the Xylos Empire.  Navigating treacherous nebulae and dodging asteroid fields, Eva faced perils far beyond any medieval traveler could imagine –  wormholes threatening to rip her ship apart, hostile alien encounters, and the constant gnawing pressure of loneliness amidst the infinite expanse of space. But the possibility of saving a lost civilization, and discovering new wonders beyond human comprehension, spurred her onward, each jump to hyperspace a thrilling leap into the unknown.
