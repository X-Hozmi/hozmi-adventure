
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she crested the pass, the wind whipping her cloak around her.  Ahead lay the rumored city of Zerzura, a fabled oasis said to hold untold riches and ancient secrets.  Her journey had been fraught with peril – bandits lurking in shadowed forests, treacherous mountain paths, and the ever-present threat of starvation and disease. But the whispers of Zerzura, the tantalizing promise of adventure, spurred her onward, a lone figure against the vast, unforgiving landscape. Each sunrise brought a fresh wave of determination, each sunset the quiet reflection of a life lived on the edge of the known world.


Centuries hence, Captain Jaxx navigated the swirling nebula of Xylos, the crimson dust of distant suns painting the cockpit in hues of fiery orange and deep violet.  His starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, pulsed with the energy of a thousand suns as it warped between galaxies, a tiny speck against the immensity of space.  His mission: to locate the lost colony of Aethel, a legend whispered across countless star systems.  The dangers were cosmic in scale – rogue asteroids, gravitational anomalies, and the ever-present threat of pirates and unknown alien entities. But the allure of discovery, the chance to rewrite history, fuelled his journey across the uncharted expanse of the cosmos.
