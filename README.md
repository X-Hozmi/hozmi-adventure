
The year is 1347.  Dust swirled around the caravan as it lumbered across the sun-baked plains, the rhythmic creak of wagon wheels a counterpoint to the hawking cries of merchants and the lowing of pack oxen.  Sir Reginald, his chainmail shimmering under the relentless sun, scanned the horizon for bandits – a constant threat on the long road to Constantinople.  Each day held the promise of new dangers, from treacherous mountain passes and raging rivers to the ever-present possibility of encountering hostile tribes.  Yet, the lure of exotic spices, silks, and untold riches pushed him onward, fuelled by the thrill of the unknown and the burning desire for adventure.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Starseeker* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her ship, a marvel of futuristic engineering, leaped between star systems with effortless grace, its trajectory plotted across light-years of space.  The vast, uncharted cosmos offered limitless possibilities – new planets teeming with life, ancient ruins whispering forgotten secrets, and the ever-present danger of encountering hostile alien civilizations.  Each jump into hyperspace was a gamble, a leap of faith into the unknown, but the thirst for discovery and the allure of galactic exploration fueled her courageous journey.
