
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onward.  Ahead lay the rumored lost city of Eldoria, a whispered legend amongst travelling merchants.  He'd traded his family's meager harvest for a rough map, a cryptic riddle etched on aged parchment promising untold riches and untold danger. Bandits, starvation, and the ever-present threat of disease were just some of the perils that stalked his journey across the unforgiving landscape, each sunrise a gamble, each sunset a testament to his grit.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, through the shimmering nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to chart unexplored sectors of the Andromeda galaxy, searching for habitable planets for humanity’s expanding empire.  The vast emptiness of space was both awe-inspiring and terrifying.  Cosmic storms threatened to tear her ship apart, alien encounters held unknown dangers, and the ever-present risk of navigational errors could mean oblivion. Yet, the vastness beckoned, a siren song of discovery echoing across the light-years.
