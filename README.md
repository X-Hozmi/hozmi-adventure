
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his weary horse onward.  His quest: to reach the fabled city of Zerzura, whispered to hold untold riches and ancient secrets.  Bandits lurked in the shadowed forests, and the endless expanse of the desert stretched before him like a cruel mirage, testing his endurance and resolve with every mile. Each sunrise brought the promise of new dangers, and each sunset the chilling reminder of his vulnerability in the vast, unforgiving wilderness.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the starship 'Stardust' through the swirling nebula of Xylos. Her mission: to establish a vital trade route between the Andromeda Galaxy and the distant Sol Federation.  Navigating treacherous asteroid fields and evading the ever-present threat of pirate factions, Eva relied on her instincts and cutting-edge technology to overcome the perils of interstellar travel. The breathtaking cosmic vistas, however, offered moments of awe-inspiring beauty amidst the constant tension of her perilous journey.
