
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's worn boots as she traversed the unforgiving plains of Aquitaine.  Her journey, a pilgrimage to Santiago de Compostela, was fraught with peril. Bandits lurked in shadowed forests, rivers swelled with spring rains, and the ever-present threat of disease hung heavy in the air.  Yet, with each sunrise, the resolute glint in her eyes reaffirmed her commitment to reach her sacred destination, her pack laden with hope and the tales of countless travelers who had gone before her.

Centuries later, Captain Ava Rostova piloted the starship *Stardust* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to chart a new hyperspace route to the Andromeda galaxy, a feat considered impossible by many.  Navigating asteroid fields and dodging the gravitational pull of rogue planets was a daily struggle, but the vast expanse of space held a different kind of allure, a thrilling unknown that beckoned her forward, pushing the boundaries of human exploration further than ever imagined.
