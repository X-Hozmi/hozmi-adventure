
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onwards.  The journey to the rumored lost city of Eldoria was proving treacherous – bandits lurked in the shadowed forests, rivers swelled with spring melt, and the endless expanse of the plains tested his endurance and the strength of his meager supplies.  Each sunrise brought new challenges, each sunset a precarious victory, but the whisper of Eldoria's riches, and the thrill of the unknown, fueled his weary steps.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a potentially habitable exoplanet light-years from Earth.  Navigating asteroid fields and battling cosmic radiation were child's play compared to the political intrigue and corporate espionage that shadowed her mission.  The vastness of space held both wonder and peril, but the potential for discovery, for expanding humanity's reach among the stars, was a siren song too powerful to resist.
