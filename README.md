Every path held the promise of both discovery and danger, whether from brigands lurking in the shadows, wild beasts guarding ancient ruins, or the unforgiving caprice of the weather. It was a time when maps were scarce, and courage was the most vital currency for any who dared to seek fortune, deliver messages, or embark on a sacred pilgrimage to distant lands.
