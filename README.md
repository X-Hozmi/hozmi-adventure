
Sir Reginald, his chainmail gleaming under the bruised twilight sky, urged his weary steed onward.  The path, a barely-there track winding through the Black Mire, promised peril but also the rumored lost city of Eldoria, said to hold untold riches and ancient magic.  Each creak of the saddle, each rustle in the reeds, sent shivers down his spine, a constant reminder of the wildness that lay beyond the meager protection of his sword and shield. Wolves howled in the distance, a chilling counterpoint to the rhythmic beat of his horse's hooves.

Captain Eva Rostova gripped the controls of the *Stardust Drifter*, her knuckles white against the polished metal.  The jump to Kepler-186f was risky, a shortcut through a volatile nebula, but time was running out. The colony on Kepler needed vital supplies, and only Eva's daring – and her ship's experimental hyperdrive – stood between them and starvation.  Nebulae danced around them in a kaleidoscope of cosmic colours, a breathtaking yet terrifying backdrop to their high-stakes gamble.
