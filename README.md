
The creaking of the wagon wheels was a constant companion, a rhythmic counterpoint to the wind whistling across the vast, uncharted plains.  Elias, his face tanned and weathered by sun and dust, squinted at the horizon.  Each day brought new challenges – bandits lurking in shadowed forests, treacherous river crossings, and the ever-present threat of starvation. But the lure of the rumored lost city of Zerzura, whispered about in hushed tones in taverns across the land, fueled his relentless journey, a beacon promising untold riches and glory. This medieval quest was a gamble with life itself, but the thrill of the unknown kept him moving forward.


A thousand years hence, Captain Ryla adjusted the course of the *Stardust Drifter*, its sleek hull a silver streak against the backdrop of the Andromeda galaxy.  Navigating the treacherous asteroid fields between the newly-charted systems was child's play compared to the bureaucratic nightmares of securing intergalactic travel permits.  The mission: retrieve a rare crystalline compound from the planet Xylos, rumored to hold the key to faster-than-light travel.   The vastness of space, while breathtaking, was also isolating; the weight of responsibility, and the potential for catastrophic failure, a constant pressure on Ryla’s shoulders.
