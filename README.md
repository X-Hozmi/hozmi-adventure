
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onward.  His quest: to reach the fabled city of Zerzura, rumored to hold untold riches and ancient secrets.  Each day brought new challenges – bandits lurking in shadowed forests, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation. Yet, the allure of the unknown, the whisper of adventure, spurred him on, a flickering candle against the vast darkness of the medieval world.


Millennia later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to locate a lost colony on the far side of the galaxy, a journey measured in light-years rather than miles.  Navigating asteroid fields, battling space pirates, and repairing critical systems under the strain of near-light speed travel was her daily bread.  The vast, awe-inspiring scale of the cosmos, however, fueled her ambition, a testament to humanity's relentless thirst for discovery across the unimaginable expanse of space.
