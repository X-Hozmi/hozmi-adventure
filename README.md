
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's worn boots as she urged her mule onward, the relentless sun baking the cracked earth of the King's Road.  Each creak of the saddle, each rustle in the parched scrub, held the potential for banditry or worse.  Her quest – to deliver a vital message to the besieged city of Aethelburg – was a race against time and the ever-present dangers of the medieval landscape: treacherous mountain passes, raging rivers, and the ever-watchful eyes of those who would exploit a lone traveler.  The journey was arduous, testing her strength and resolve at every turn, but the thought of her destination fueled her onward.


Centuries later, Captain Rexx slammed his fist on the console, the stars streaking past the viewport of the *Stardust Drifter*.  Their jump to Kepler-186f had been rough, throwing the crew against the bulkhead, but they were finally within range of the promising exoplanet.  The galactic map displayed countless nebulae and uncharted systems, each jump a gamble against the unknown dangers of hyperspace, pirate raids, and the ever-present threat of navigational errors that could leave them stranded in the void. This was a journey of scientific discovery, pushing the boundaries of human exploration into the unfathomable expanse of the cosmos.
