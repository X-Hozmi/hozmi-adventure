
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Sir Kaelan spurred his weary horse onward.  His quest: to reach the mythical city of Eldoria, rumored to hold a cure for the plague ravaging his homeland.  Days bled into weeks, each sunrise revealing a new landscape – dense forests teeming with bandits, treacherous mountain passes guarded by snow leopards, and sprawling plains where the sun beat down mercilessly.  Each mile was a gamble, a test of endurance against the elements and the unknown perils lurking in the shadows.  The promise of Eldoria, however faint, kept his spirit alight.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the 'Stardust Drifter' through the swirling nebula of Xylos. Her mission: to locate a lost colony on the far side of the Andromeda galaxy.  Navigating asteroid fields and battling temporal distortions were commonplace hazards.  Communication with Earth was sporadic at best, the vast distances stretching trust and patience thin. Yet, the hope of finding survivors, of connecting with a piece of humanity lost to the void, fueled her relentless journey through the uncharted cosmos.  The galaxy stretched before her, a breathtaking, terrifying tapestry of wonder and danger.
