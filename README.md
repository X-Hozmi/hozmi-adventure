
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she navigated the treacherous mountain pass, the jagged peaks scraping the bruised twilight sky.  Her worn map, a testament to countless revisions and tear-stained corrections, offered little comfort against the howling wind.  Ahead lay the rumored city of Eldoria, said to hold the key to her family's lost legacy – a journey fraught with peril, bandits, and the ever-present threat of starvation, yet Elara pressed on, fueled by a stubborn hope that flickered like a candle in the storm.


Centuries hence, Captain Rexx "Razor" Rylan steered the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos, his breath fogging in the low-gravity cockpit.  His destination: Kepler-186f, a planet promising untold riches and equally untold dangers.  Ahead lay the vast emptiness of space, punctuated by the shimmering trails of rival starships and the ominous whispers of pirate broadcasts.  The journey was a gamble, a high-stakes race against time and ruthless competitors, but the allure of a new world, a new frontier, outweighed the risk for the intrepid captain and his crew.
