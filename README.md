
The year is 1347.  Dust swirled around Sir Reginald's worn boots as he urged his weary horse onward.  The journey across the treacherous Black Forest was proving far more challenging than anticipated.  Bandits lurked in the shadows, the trail was barely discernible, and the gnawing hunger was a constant companion.  Yet, the whispered legends of the lost city of Eldoria, said to hold untold riches and ancient magic, spurred him on.  Each creak of the saddle, each rustle in the undergrowth, fueled the thrill of the unknown and the promise of glory.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted her starship *Stardust* through the shimmering nebula of Xylos.  The jump to the Kepler-186f system had been risky, pushing the ship's Alcubierre drive to its limits.  Ahead lay a potentially habitable planet, but also the uncharted dangers of a galaxy teeming with alien life – both benevolent and hostile.  Navigating asteroid fields, evading space pirates, and deciphering cryptic alien signals were all part of the day’s work for a galaxy-hopping explorer. The vastness of space, a daunting emptiness, held far greater and stranger adventures than any medieval forest ever could.
