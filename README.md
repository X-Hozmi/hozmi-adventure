
The wind whipped through the frayed edges of the traveler's cloak, carrying the scent of pine and damp earth as he trudged along the muddy road. The year was 1347, and the world stretched before him, a tapestry of rolling hills, ancient forests, and bustling market towns. Rumors of a lost city whispered on the wind, beckoning him westward, past the Black Forest and across the untamed steppes. Danger lurked at every turn – bandits, wild beasts, and the ever-present threat of disease – but the promise of adventure fueled his weary steps.

A thousand years hence, the stars themselves became the roads. Captain Anya, with her crew of hardened spacefarers, navigated a vast nebula, the vibrant colours of the nebula swirling around their sleek vessel. Their mission: to map a new sector of the galaxy, charting the course for future expeditions. Beyond the familiar constellations lay untold worlds, some teeming with life, others barren and desolate. But the unknown called to them, a siren song of exploration and discovery, urging them onward into the boundless expanse of space.
