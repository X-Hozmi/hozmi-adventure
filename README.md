
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she traversed the sun-baked plains, the scent of thyme and sweat heavy in the air.  Her worn leather satchel held her meager possessions and a map, its creases mirroring the lines etched on her weathered face.  Each sunrise brought the promise of bandits, treacherous terrain, and the ever-present threat of starvation, but also the allure of the legendary Sunstone city rumored to lie beyond the Whispering Mountains – a city said to hold the key to ending the crippling famine that gripped her homeland.  The journey was arduous, a test of both physical and mental fortitude, yet the hope of salvation fueled her weary steps.


Centuries later, Captain Rexx "Razor" Rylan piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos, the crimson dust reflecting in the viewport. His destination: Kepler-186f, a potential haven for humanity struggling to survive a dying Earth.  Navigating the treacherous asteroid fields and avoiding the territorial claims of the Kryll Empire was a constant challenge, but the potential discovery of a new home – a new beginning – outweighed the risks.  The vastness of space, both beautiful and terrifying, offered a constant reminder of the fragility of life and the immense courage required to venture beyond the known.
