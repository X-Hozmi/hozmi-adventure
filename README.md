
The year is 1347.  Dust billows behind Elara's mule as she crests the pass, the wind whipping her cloak around her like a frantic bird.  Ahead stretches the endless expanse of the Rhône valley, promising both bounty and peril.  Her worn map, a patchwork of faded ink and whispered legends, guides her towards the rumored lost city of Eldoria, a journey fraught with brigands, treacherous terrain, and the ever-present threat of starvation.  Each sunrise brings a new challenge, each sunset a hard-won victory, forging her into a survivor amidst the raw beauty and unforgiving nature of medieval Europe.


Conversely, Captain Ryla's starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, leaps across the void between galaxies.  Navigating treacherous nebulae and dodging the gravitational pull of black holes is a daily routine for her and her crew.  Their mission: to chart the unmapped regions of the Andromeda galaxy, seeking out new worlds teeming with life, or perhaps, even ancient civilizations lost to time.  The vastness of space, while breathtaking, is also isolating; the weight of their mission, immense. Yet, the possibility of discovery fuels their relentless pursuit across the cosmos.
