Travel across land was a perilous yet necessary undertaking, driven by crusades, pilgrimages, trade, or the simple human desire for discovery. With every step away from the safety of familiar walls, one ventured into a landscape ripe with both danger and the promise of untold wonders, where every horizon held a new secret waiting to be unveiled.
