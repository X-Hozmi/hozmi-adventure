Each horizon promised unknown villages, feudal lords, opportunistic bandits, or perhaps even a mythical beast glimpsed in the deep woods. Supplies were sparse, maps crude, and every day a test of endurance against the elements and human nature, where survival often hinged on wit, strength, and a measure of luck.

Yet, it was this very peril that fueled the spirit of adventure.
