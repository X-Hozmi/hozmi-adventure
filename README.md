
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the mud-caked track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onward.  The journey from York to Canterbury had already claimed two of his companions, felled by fever and banditry respectively.  Each sunrise brought the gnawing fear of the unknown – treacherous bogs, marauding wolves, and the ever-present threat of highwaymen.  Yet, the promise of a renowned master craftsman in Canterbury, and the whispered tales of ancient secrets held within its cathedral, spurred him onward, a flicker of hope against the bleak landscape.


Conversely, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos, the vibrant colours a stark contrast to the sterile steel of her cockpit.  Her mission: to retrieve a vital data core from the abandoned mining colony on Kepler-186f, a planet bathed in the crimson light of its dying sun.  Navigating asteroid fields and dodging the occasional pirate vessel was all in a day's work, but the true danger lay in the unpredictable gravitational currents and the whispers of ancient, malevolent entities said to haunt the fringes of known space. The galaxy offered unimaginable riches, but demanded a price for its secrets.
