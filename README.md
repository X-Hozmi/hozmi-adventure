
Sir Gareth, his armor gleaming under the relentless sun, urged his weary steed onward.  The trail, barely more than a goat track, snaked through the unforgiving peaks of the Dragon's Tooth mountains.  Ahead lay the rumored city of Eldoria, a jewel whispered to hold untold riches and ancient magic, but also rumored to be guarded by creatures of nightmare.  Each creak of his saddle, each rustle in the undergrowth, sharpened his senses, fueling the potent cocktail of fear and excitement that defined his perilous journey.  He was a knight errant, seeking not glory, but the cure for his ailing sister.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the 'Starseeker' through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a planet promising a new beginning for a generation fleeing Earth's ravaged surface. The jump to hyperspace had been brutal, a jarring shift between realities that left the crew shaken, but the thrill of interstellar travel, of pushing the boundaries of human exploration, outweighed the risks.  Ahead lay not mountains and monsters, but the unknown, the infinite possibilities of a new world waiting to be discovered.
