
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she traversed the sun-baked plains, her worn leather jerkin shielding her from the relentless heat.  Ahead lay the rumored City of Whispers, a legendary metropolis said to hold untold riches and ancient secrets.  Each creak of the saddle, each rustle in the tall grass, held the promise of danger – bandits, wild beasts, and the ever-present threat of starvation.  Yet, the allure of the unknown propelled her onward, a lone adventurer against the vast, unforgiving landscape.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the starship *Stardust* across the void between galaxies. The swirling nebulae painted breathtaking canvases outside her viewport, a stark contrast to the sterile hum of the ship's engines.  Her mission: to locate the fabled Xylos system, a legendary nexus of advanced civilizations.  Navigation through asteroid fields and the ever-present risk of encountering hostile alien species presented a different kind of peril, but the boundless expanse of space, brimming with possibilities, fueled her relentless pursuit.
