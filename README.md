
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she traversed the sun-baked plains of Provence, her worn map clutched tight.  Rumors of a hidden monastery, rumored to hold ancient secrets and untold riches, spurred her onward.  Bandits, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of disease were mere inconveniences compared to the lure of the unknown. Each sunrise brought a new challenge, each sunset a breathtaking vista – a testament to the unpredictable beauty and peril of medieval overland travel.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the 'Stardust Wanderer' across the vast, inky expanse between galaxies.  Her destination: Xylos, a planet whispered to possess a powerful, life-sustaining energy source.  Navigating asteroid fields, dodging space pirates, and battling the crushing loneliness of interstellar travel were all part of the daily grind. Yet, the thrill of discovery, the potential to unlock unimaginable advancements for humanity, far outweighed the risks.  The cosmos, a boundless ocean of wonder and danger, awaited.
