
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Sir Reginald's weary steed as he crested the next hill, the setting sun painting the unforgiving landscape in hues of blood orange and bruised purple. His quest – to reach the legendary lost city of Eldoria, rumored to hold a cure for the Black Death ravaging his homeland – felt increasingly hopeless.  Each day brought new challenges: treacherous mountain passes, skirmishes with bandits, and the gnawing hunger in his belly. Yet, the whispered tales of Eldoria's healing waters fueled his determination, pushing him onward through the vast, unpredictable wilderness.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the 'Stardust Drifter' through the swirling nebula of Xylos, her heart thrumming with a different kind of anticipation.  Ahead lay Kepler-186f, a planet promising untold riches and scientific breakthroughs. But the journey was far from safe; pirate raids in the outer rim were common, and the unpredictable gravitational forces of the nebula presented a constant threat. Yet, the allure of the unknown, the potential for discovery that lay beyond the stars, outweighed the dangers a thousandfold.  The vastness of space, while terrifying, was also incredibly exhilarating.
