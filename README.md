
The year is 1347.  Dust billows behind Elara's worn boots as she trudges across the sun-baked plains, her pack heavy with meager supplies.  Ahead, the jagged peaks of the Dragon's Teeth mountains loom, a formidable barrier between her and the fabled city of Eldoria.  Bandits, starvation, and the ever-present threat of disease are her constant companions, yet the whispers of Eldoria's lost magic fuel her onward. Each sunrise brings a new challenge, a new landscape, a new test of her resilience in this unforgiving medieval world.

Conversely, Captain Jaxx navigates the shimmering nebula of Xylos in 2742, his starship, the 'Stardust Wanderer,' a tiny speck against the cosmic canvas.  His mission: to locate the lost colony of Kepler-186f, rumored to hold the key to interstellar peace.  Hyperspace jumps are fraught with danger – rogue asteroids, temporal anomalies, and the ever-present threat of pirate raiders.  Yet, the vastness of space, the breathtaking beauty of nebulas, and the promise of discovery keep him pushing forward, a lone voyager charting unknown galaxies.
