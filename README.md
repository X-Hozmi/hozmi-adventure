
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his pack heavy with meager supplies, urged his weary horse onward.  He’d left the familiar fields of his village months ago, driven by whispers of a legendary lost city said to hold untold riches. Each day was a gamble against bandits, starvation, and the ever-present threat of disease.  The towering castles and bustling towns he passed were only fleeting glimpses of a world vastly different from his own, each encounter a new story etched into the tapestry of his adventure.  The road stretched endlessly ahead, a path paved with both peril and the intoxicating allure of the unknown.


Conversely, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the shimmering nebula of Xylos, its vibrant colours a stark contrast to the cold, calculating efficiency of her ship's systems.  Her mission: to chart a course through unmapped sectors of the Andromeda galaxy, searching for habitable planets for humanity's expanding reach.  Each jump through hyperspace was a leap of faith, a gamble against unforeseen gravitational anomalies and hostile alien life. The vast emptiness of space held its own terrors, as isolating as any medieval wilderness, but the promise of discovery and the thrill of the unknown fueled her relentless journey among the stars.
