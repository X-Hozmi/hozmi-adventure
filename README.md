
The year is 1347.  Dust swirled around the worn leather boots of Thomas as he urged his weary horse onward.  The sun beat down on the cracked earth of the King's Road, a ribbon of grey cutting through the endless fields of wheat.  His quest: to reach the distant monastery of St. Jude, rumored to possess a relic capable of healing his ailing sister.  Each creak of the saddle, each rustle in the nearby woods, held the promise of both peril and discovery – bandits, wild beasts, and the ever-present threat of disease were constant companions on his arduous journey across the fractured landscape of medieval Europe.


Centuries later, Captain Elara Reyes piloted the starship *Stardust* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to chart the unexplored sectors of the Andromeda galaxy, searching for habitable planets suitable for human colonization. The vastness of space was both terrifying and exhilarating.  Navigating asteroid fields, dodging rogue comets, and communicating with alien civilizations were everyday challenges.  The reward? The potential to expand humanity's reach across the cosmos and uncover secrets older than time itself.
