
Sir Reginald, his worn leather jerkin creaking with each step, urged his destrier onward.  Dust devils danced across the sun-baked plains of Eldoria, mirroring the turmoil in his own heart.  He was on a quest, a perilous journey to the fabled city of Zerzura, rumored to hold the lost scriptures of the Ancients.  Bandits, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation were but small obstacles in his pursuit of glory and the potential salvation of his kingdom. Each sunrise brought a new challenge, each sunset a renewed determination to press on.


Millennia later, Captain Eva Rostova navigated her starship, the *Stardust Wanderer*, through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to retrieve a vital energy source from the remote planet Kepler-186f, a world teeming with both breathtaking beauty and deadly xenofauna.  Faster-than-light travel was a constant gamble, the unpredictable nature of hyperspace a terrifying reminder of the vast, unforgiving expanse of the cosmos. But the need to secure the energy source, vital for the survival of her colony, outweighed the risks, pushing Eva and her crew ever onward into the uncharted territories of the galaxy.
