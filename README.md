
Sir Reginald, his worn leather jerkin creaking with every stride, spurred his destrier onward.  The chill wind whipped across the desolate moor, carrying the scent of pine and the distant, unsettling cries of wolves.  His quest, to reach the mythical city of Eldoria and retrieve the Sunstone, led him across treacherous mountain passes, through echoing forests haunted by brigands, and across rivers swollen with the spring thaw. Each day brought fresh peril, each night the gnawing fear of the unknown, yet the lure of glory and the whispered promise of the Sunstone's power kept him pushing ever forward.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the shimmering nebula of Xylos.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a potentially habitable exoplanet light-years from Earth.  Navigating asteroid fields, dodging the gravitational pull of rogue planets, and battling the ever-present threat of space pirates were all in a day's work for a seasoned galactic explorer.  But the allure of discovery, the hope of finding life beyond Earth, and the promise of untold scientific advancements fueled her journey across the vast, uncharted expanse of the cosmos.
