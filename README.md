
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's horse as she urged it onward, the setting sun painting the unforgiving landscape of the Pyrenees in hues of blood orange and bruised purple.  Her worn leather satchel held little beyond dried meats, a worn map patched with prayer, and the hope of finding her estranged brother in the distant city of Avignon.  Bandits, treacherous passes, and the ever-present threat of disease were constant companions on her arduous journey, each day a gamble against fate and the unforgiving terrain.  The adventure wasn't glamorous, but it was hers, a testament to the unwavering bond of family.


Conversely, Captain Rexa of the starship *Stardust Drifter* navigated the swirling nebula of Xylos, her sensors picking up faint signals from a long-lost colony on the far side of the Andromeda galaxy.  The journey across light years was measured not in days or weeks, but in generations, a slow crawl through the vast emptiness between worlds.  While the dangers were different –  cosmic storms, pirate raids from other galaxies, and the ever-present threat of system malfunction –  the spirit of adventure remained the same;  a relentless pursuit of the unknown, fuelled by a human curiosity that transcended time and space.
