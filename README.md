
The year is 1347.  Dust choked the air as Sir Gareth's worn leather boots pounded the rutted track, his squire struggling to keep pace.  Their journey across the treacherous Pyrenees was a tapestry woven with peril: bandits lurking in shadowed passes, the relentless sting of mountain winds, and the constant gnawing hunger. Yet, the glint of the distant sun reflecting off the spires of the rumored lost city spurred them onward, a promise of untold riches and ancient secrets justifying every blister and aching muscle.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova navigated the swirling nebula of Xylos, her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, a tiny speck against the cosmic canvas.  The jump to the Andromeda galaxy had been treacherous, pushing the ship's engines to their limits, and the alien signals they'd intercepted hinted at unknown dangers awaiting them on the planet Kepler-186f.  But the quest for the legendary Xylos Crystal, a source of limitless energy, fueled their determination, a thirst for discovery that transcended time and space.
