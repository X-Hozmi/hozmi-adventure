
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onward.  Ahead lay the rumored lost city of Eldoria, whispered to hold treasures beyond imagining.  Bandits, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation were all obstacles in his quest, each sunrise presenting a new challenge and a chance for glory or ruin. The weight of his worn map, promising riches and adventure, felt lighter than the hope that burned in his heart.

Centuries later,  Captain Eva Rostova piloted the 'Stardust Drifter' through a nebula shimmering with iridescent hues.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a potential Earth-like planet light years from Sol.  Navigating asteroid fields, battling space pirates, and managing the fragile ecosystem of her ship were commonplace hazards on this intergalactic voyage. The vastness of space, both terrifying and beautiful, held the promise of discovery and the unknown, a new frontier for human ambition.
