
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the open plains as Sir Kaelan, his armor gleaming faintly in the weak sun, urged his weary horse onward.  Their journey from the King's court to the far north was proving far more arduous than anticipated.  Bandits lurked in the shadows of ancient forests, rivers swelled with spring rains blocked their path, and the ever-present threat of starvation hung heavy in the air. Yet, the promise of the legendary Sunstone, said to hold the power to heal the blighted lands, fueled his relentless pursuit, each day a gamble against the elements and the unknown.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the 'Stardust Wanderer' through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to deliver vital terraforming enzymes to Kepler-186f, a planet teetering on the brink of collapse.  The journey across galaxies was a symphony of calculated risks – navigating asteroid fields, evading pirate fleets, and battling the unpredictable whims of hyperspace. But the stakes were higher than ever before; the survival of a nascent colony, a beacon of hope amidst the vast, indifferent cosmos, rested on her shoulders and the reliability of her aging starship.
