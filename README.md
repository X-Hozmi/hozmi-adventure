
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onward.  The scent of woodsmoke and damp earth hung heavy in the air, a stark contrast to the tales whispered in taverns of exotic spices and shimmering silks from the East.  His quest, to find a lost monastery rumored to hold ancient secrets, was a perilous one; bandits lurked in the shadows of every forest, and the endless expanse of unfamiliar lands promised both wonder and unimaginable hardship. Each sunrise brought new challenges, each sunset the gnawing uncertainty of survival.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova gripped the controls of the *Stardust Drifter*, the vibrant nebula of Xylos swirling outside her viewport.  Her mission: to chart a newly discovered wormhole leading to a galaxy teeming with unmapped planets.  The thrill of exploration pulsed through her veins, tempered by the knowledge of the immense distances and unpredictable dangers inherent in interstellar travel.  Each jump through hyperspace was a gamble, each newly encountered system a potential treasure trove of wonders or a deadly trap. The vastness of space held both infinite promise and the chilling possibility of utter isolation.
