
The year is 1347.  Dust coats Sir Reginald's worn leather jerkin as he guides his weary horse across the sun-baked plains.  His quest: to reach the legendary city of Zerzura, rumored to hold a cure for the plague ravaging his homeland.  Each sunrise brings the threat of bandits, treacherous terrain, and the gnawing uncertainty of the journey.  Yet, with every step, the allure of the unknown, the whispers of ancient prophecies, and the desperate hope for salvation fuel his arduous trek across the unforgiving landscape.

Alternatively,  Captain Eva Rostova pilots the starship *Odyssey* through the swirling nebula of Xylos, her destination a distant galaxy harboring a rumored technological marvel capable of saving her dying planet.  Years have passed since she left Earth, each jump through hyperspace a gamble against unforeseen celestial anomalies.  Alien landscapes and hostile encounters punctuate the voyage, forcing her and her crew to constantly adapt and innovate to survive.  The vastness of space, the relentless pressure of time, and the promise of a future for her people drive her onward through the cosmic void.
