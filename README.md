
The year is 1347.  Sir Reginald, his worn leather jerkin creaking with every stride, spurred his destrier onward.  Dust swirled around them, kicked up by the hooves of his small company as they traversed the unforgiving terrain.  Their quest: to reach the fabled city of Eldoria, rumored to hold the lost secrets of a powerful magic.  Bandits, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation shadowed their every step, but the glint of adventure, a beacon brighter than any star, kept their spirits high and their swords sharp.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the starship 'Stardust Drifter' through the shimmering nebula of Xylos.  Her crew, a diverse band of explorers from across the known galaxies, charted unknown systems, seeking out new worlds and valuable resources.  The hum of the warp drive was a constant companion, punctuated by the crackle of comms and the thrill of discovering new life forms – some friendly, some terrifyingly alien.  Their voyage, a perilous dance amongst celestial bodies, was a testament to humanity’s unending thirst for exploration beyond the confines of their known universe.
