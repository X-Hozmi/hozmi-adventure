
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his weary horse onward.  The scent of woodsmoke and damp earth filled the air, a stark contrast to the rumors of opulent silks and exotic spices that fueled his journey east.  Each creak of the saddle, each ford across a rushing stream, represented a step closer to the legendary city of Samarkand, and a step further from the familiar confines of his village. Bandits, treacherous terrain, and the ever-present threat of disease were constant companions on this perilous, yet exhilarating, adventure.


Millennia later, Captain Eva Rostova gripped the controls of her starship, the *Stardust Wanderer*, the vast, swirling nebula of Xylos painting the viewport in vibrant hues.  Her mission: to chart the unexplored sectors of the Andromeda Galaxy, a journey spanning light years and fraught with the unpredictable dangers of unmapped wormholes and hostile alien civilizations.  Each warp jump was a leap of faith, a gamble against the unknown, but the allure of discovery, the promise of encountering new worlds and life forms beyond human comprehension, fueled her unwavering resolve.
