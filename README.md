
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onward.  Ahead lay the rumored city of Eldoria, a place whispered about in hushed tones – a city of gold and magic, guarded by monstrous beasts and treacherous terrain.  Each creak of the saddle, each rustle in the dense forest, fueled a mixture of fear and thrilling anticipation.  He carried with him not only a meager supply of dried meat and hardtack, but also a burning desire for adventure, for a life beyond the drudgery of his village.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the starship *Stardust* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a planet rumored to hold the key to interstellar travel's next great leap.  The vastness of space, punctuated by the twinkling of distant galaxies, was both awe-inspiring and terrifying.  Facing radiation storms, navigating asteroid fields, and the ever-present threat of pirate ships, Eva and her crew pushed the boundaries of human exploration, driven by the insatiable human hunger for the unknown and the promise of discoveries beyond imagination.
