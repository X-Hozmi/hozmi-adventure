
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onward.  The scent of woodsmoke and damp earth filled his nostrils, a stark contrast to the tales whispered in taverns of exotic spices and silks from the far East.  His quest, to reach the mythical city of Samarkand, was a perilous one, fraught with bandit raids, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of disease.  Each sunrise brought a new challenge, a new test of his resilience and determination to reach his goal, whatever treasures or dangers it held.


Centuries later, Elara’s starship, the *Stardust Wanderer*, cut a shimmering path through the nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to chart unexplored sectors of the Andromeda galaxy, searching for habitable planets.  The vast emptiness of space, punctuated by the breathtaking beauty of celestial wonders, both thrilled and humbled her.  Each jump to hyperspace was a gamble, a leap of faith into the unknown.  The potential discovery of new life, new worlds, fuelled her courage, overshadowing the ever-present risks of encountering hostile alien civilizations or encountering unforeseen technological malfunctions in the deep black.
