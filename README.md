
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara’s worn boots as she urged her mule onward, the setting sun painting the rugged, unforgiving landscape in hues of blood orange and bruised purple.  Her journey across the Holy Roman Empire was fraught with peril – bandits lurked in shadowed forests, treacherous mountain passes tested her strength and resolve, and the ever-present threat of disease hung heavy in the air. Yet, with each sunrise, the allure of the rumored lost city of Eldoria, whispered of in hushed tones by grizzled travelers, fueled her onward. The promise of untold riches and ancient knowledge spurred her across the unforgiving miles.

Alternatively, Captain Jaxx slammed his fist on the console, the jolt reverberating through the bridge of the *Stardust Drifter*.  Their hyperspace jump had been bumpy, spitting them out near the edge of the Andromeda Galaxy, light-years from their intended destination.  Navigating the treacherous asteroid fields ahead was a hair-raising task, each rogue rock a potential death sentence.  The galactic empire’s strict trade routes were miles away, leaving them vulnerable.  But the promise of the fabled Xylos crystals, said to hold unimaginable power, drove them forward through the inky black, a perilous but potentially lucrative voyage into the unexplored reaches of space.
