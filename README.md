
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she traversed the sun-baked plains, her worn leather jerkin offering little protection from the relentless heat.  Ahead lay the rumored city of Zerzura, a fabled oasis said to hold untold riches and ancient secrets. Bandits, treacherous terrain, and the ever-present threat of starvation were her constant companions, but the lure of the unknown spurred her onward, each sunrise a fresh gamble against the unforgiving landscape.  Every creak of the saddle, every distant hawk cry, fueled the burning hope in her heart.

Centuries later, Captain Jax navigated the iridescent nebula of Xylos, his starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, a tiny speck against the cosmic canvas.  Faster-than-light travel was commonplace, but the dangers of hyperspace jumps were still real – rogue asteroids, gravitational anomalies, and the ever-present threat of pirate raiders. The mission: to retrieve a vital energy source from the planet Kepler-186f, a world shrouded in mystery and rumored to hold ancient alien technology. The vastness of space was daunting, yet the thrill of exploration, the promise of discovery, outweighed any fear.
