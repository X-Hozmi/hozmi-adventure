
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's horse as she urged it onward, the setting sun painting the endless plains of France in fiery hues. Her journey to the Holy Land was fraught with peril – bandits lurked in shadowed forests, rivers swelled with treacherous currents, and the ever-present threat of disease hung heavy in the air.  Each sunrise brought new challenges, from navigating unfamiliar terrain to bartering for meager provisions in sparsely populated villages.  Yet, the unwavering faith in her heart, and the whispered tales of Jerusalem's sacred sites, spurred her relentlessly forward.


Centuries later, Captain Rexx "Razor" Raxon piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  His mission: to retrieve a vital bio-sample from the ice planet of Kepler-186f, a journey spanning light-years and fraught with the unpredictable dangers of hyperspace.  Navigating asteroid fields, evading pirate scavengers, and battling the crippling effects of prolonged warp travel were all part of a day's work. The vast, uncharted expanse of the cosmos offered breathtaking beauty, but also held the potential for catastrophic failure – a single miscalculation could mean the end of his crew and his ship, lost forever amongst the glittering dust of the galaxies.
