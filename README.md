
The year is 1347.  A chill wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his pack heavy with meager supplies, urged his weary horse onward.  His quest: to reach the mythical city of Ys, whispered to hold the secrets of lost alchemy.  Each creaking wooden bridge, each shadowy forest, held the potential for banditry, starvation, or worse – the fever that stalked the land. Yet, the lure of the unknown, the whispered legends, spurred him on, a single knight errant against the vast and unforgiving landscape of medieval Europe.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova navigated her starship *Stardust* through the swirling nebula of Xylos. Her mission: to locate a rumored source of Xylium, a rare element crucial to faster-than-light travel.  The vastness of space, punctuated by shimmering constellations and the constant hum of the ship's engines, was both breathtaking and terrifying.  Every jump across hyperspace held the potential for navigational errors, pirate attacks, or the insidious effects of prolonged exposure to the unknown energies of the cosmos. But the promise of scientific advancement, and the thrill of charting unmapped galaxies, fueled her relentless journey.
