
The year is 1347.  Sir Reginald, his surcoat mud-caked and his sword gleaming faintly in the afternoon sun, urged his weary steed onward.  The journey across the treacherous Black Forest had been brutal – bandits, treacherous bogs, and the ever-present threat of wolves had tested his resolve and his men to their limits. Yet, the whispered tales of a lost city, brimming with forgotten magic and untold riches, fueled his relentless pursuit, each aching muscle a testament to the adventure that lay just beyond the horizon.


Millennia later, Captain Elara Jax navigated the shimmering nebula of Xylos, her starship, the *Stardust Wanderer*, a tiny speck against the cosmic canvas.  The jump to the Andromeda galaxy had been risky, pushing the ship's hyperdrive to its absolute limit.  Ahead lay Kepler-186f, a planet rumored to hold the key to unlocking faster-than-light travel – a prize worth risking everything for, even the unforgiving expanse of interstellar space itself.
