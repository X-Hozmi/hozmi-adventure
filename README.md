
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she crested the pass, the wind whipping her cloak around her like a frantic bird.  Before her stretched the vast, untamed expanse of the Rhône valley, promising both bounty and peril.  Rumors of a hidden monastery, rumored to hold ancient healing secrets, spurred her onward, despite the ever-present threat of bandits, unpredictable weather, and the gnawing hunger in her belly. Each sunrise brought a new challenge, each sunset a renewed sense of determination to reach her destination, forging a path through the harsh beauty of medieval France.


Centuries later, Captain Jaxx navigated the shimmering nebula, the emerald glow of Xylos reflected in the viewport of the 'Stardust Drifter'.  His mission: to retrieve a vital data core from the remote planet of Aethel, a world shrouded in mystery and guarded by sentient, crystalline flora.  Warp jumps strained the ship's engines, and the alien landscapes, from volcanic plains to bioluminescent oceans, posed constant threats.  The journey wasn't just a matter of traversing space; it was a gamble against unknown dangers and the ever-present possibility of encountering civilizations far more advanced, and potentially hostile, than their own.
