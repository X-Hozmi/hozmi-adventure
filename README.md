
The year is 1347.  Sir Reginald, his worn leather jerkin clinging to his sweat-soaked back, spurred his destrier onward.  The dust kicked up by their hooves painted the late afternoon sun a bruised purple.  Ahead lay the treacherous Black Mire, rumored to swallow men and beasts whole, a necessary evil on the route to the Sultan's court.  His quest – to retrieve a stolen relic – was a perilous dance with fate, each league a gamble against bandits, treacherous terrain, and the ever-present gnawing hunger.  The hope of glory, however, spurred him on.


Centuries hence, Captain Eva Rostova gripped the controls of her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*.  Beyond the viewport, a swirling nebula painted the void in hues unseen on any world.  Her mission: to chart the newly discovered Xylos system, a cluster of planets teeming with alien flora and fauna, some friendly, others... decidedly not.  The jump drive hummed, a constant thrumming reminder of the immense distances and the potential dangers lurking in the vast, unexplored reaches of the galaxy.  The thrill of discovery, however, outweighed the risk.
