
The year is 1347.  Dust billows behind Sir Kaelan's steed as he rides across the sun-baked plains of Aquitaine.  His quest: to reach the mythical city of Eldoria, rumored to hold a cure for the plague ravaging his homeland.  Bandits, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation are constant companions.  Each sunrise brings new perils, but the hope of salvation fuels his relentless journey, forging him into a hardened warrior tempered by hardship and unwavering resolve.


Centuries later,  Captain Eva Rostova pilots the 'Starseeker' through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to locate the lost colony of Kepler-186f, a planet swallowed by a temporal anomaly.  Navigating treacherous asteroid fields and dodging the predatory Voidhawks, Eva relies on her trusty crew and cutting-edge technology to overcome the vast distances and unimaginable dangers of interstellar travel.  The fate of countless lives rests on the success of her perilous expedition across the galaxy.
