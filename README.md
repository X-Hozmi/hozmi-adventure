
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she crested the hill, the vast, unforgiving landscape of the French countryside stretching before her.  Her worn leather satchel held little beyond a meager supply of dried meat and a well-thumbed map, its creases mirroring the lines etched onto her face by years of travel.  Ahead lay the rumored city of Avalon, a legendary haven whispered about in hushed tones – a perilous journey through bandit-infested forests and treacherous mountain passes, but a destination worth risking everything for.  Each creak of the mule's saddle, each rustle in the undergrowth, was a reminder of the unpredictable dangers that awaited.


Captain Eva Rostova gripped the controls of the *Stardust Drifter*, the twin suns of Kepler-16b casting long shadows across the bridge.  Their mission: to chart the unmapped nebula of Xylos, a swirling vortex of cosmic dust and untold wonders, rumored to hold the key to faster-than-light travel.  The journey would take them across light-years of empty space, facing the perils of solar flares, rogue asteroids, and the ever-present threat of unknown alien entities.  But the allure of discovery, the chance to expand humanity's reach across the galaxy, was a siren song too powerful to resist.
