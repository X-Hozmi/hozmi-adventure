
The year is 1348.  A biting wind whipped across the plains as Sir Reginald, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his weary steed onward.  Their journey, a pilgrimage to the fabled shrine of Saint Jude, had taken them through treacherous mountain passes, across sun-baked deserts teeming with bandits, and through forests whispering with the secrets of ancient druids.  Each sunrise brought new peril, from river crossings swollen with spring melt to the ever-present threat of disease, but the lure of salvation spurred them on, their meager supplies dwindling with every league conquered.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the iridescent nebula of Xylos.  The journey to the Kepler-186f colony had already consumed months, traversing wormholes and navigating asteroid fields, but the breathtaking cosmic vistas offered a constant reward.  The challenges were different now – engine malfunctions requiring swift repairs under the watchful gaze of alien sentinels, navigating shifting gravitational fields, and the ever-present risk of encountering hostile pirate fleets. Yet, the promise of a new life on a terraformed planet, teeming with unknown wonders, fueled her determination to reach her destination.
