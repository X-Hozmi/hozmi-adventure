
The year is 1347.  Dust swirled around Elara's worn boots as she urged her horse onward, the setting sun casting long shadows across the unforgiving landscape.  Their journey to the fabled city of Zerzura was fraught with peril – bandits lurking in canyons, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation.  Each sunrise brought a new challenge, a new test of their endurance, but the whispered promise of Zerzura's legendary riches and hidden knowledge fueled their weary steps.  The scent of woodsmoke from a distant village offered a fleeting comfort before the endless plains swallowed them whole once more.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the starship *Stardust*, its sleek hull a silver splinter against the inky backdrop of the Andromeda galaxy.  Navigation across the vast interstellar distances was a constant dance with gravity wells and nebulae, each jump a gamble.  Her crew, a diverse band of explorers, faced the unknown with a mix of exhilaration and apprehension.  The mission: to chart unexplored star systems and search for habitable planets, a quest far riskier than any medieval pilgrimage, yet mirroring its inherent allure of discovery and the boundless human thirst for the uncharted.
