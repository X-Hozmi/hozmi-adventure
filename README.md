
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she traversed the unforgiving landscape of the French countryside.  Each creak of the saddle, each rustle in the overgrown fields, held the potential for banditry or worse.  Her quest: to reach the distant monastery rumored to hold the cure for her ailing brother.  Days bled into weeks, filled with the camaraderie of fellow travelers, the fear of the unknown, and the constant, gnawing hunger.  The tapestry of medieval life – its hardships and unpredictable beauty – unfolded with every sunrise and sunset.


Millennia later, Captain Eva Rostova steered the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a planet rumored to hold the key to interdimensional travel.  The journey was fraught with peril: rogue asteroids, unpredictable wormholes, and the ever-present threat of pirate vessels. But the allure of discovery, the potential to unlock the universe's secrets, fueled her crew's tireless efforts. The vastness of space, both terrifying and exhilarating, was a constant companion on their epic voyage.
