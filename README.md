
The creaking of the wagon wheels was a constant companion, a rhythmic counterpoint to the wind whistling through the gaps in the worn canvas.  Sir Kaelan, his armor dulled by sun and dust, squinted at the horizon.  Weeks had bled into months since leaving Camelot, each sunrise revealing a new challenge – bandits lurking in shadowed forests, treacherous mountain passes choked with snow, and unforgiving stretches of barren plains where water was as precious as gold.  Yet, the thrill of the unknown, the whispered legends of the mythical city of Eldoria, fueled his weary body and spurred him onward.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova navigated the swirling nebula, the vast expanse of the Andromeda Galaxy a breathtaking, terrifying backdrop to her journey.  Her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, hummed with the energy of a thousand suns, a fragile vessel against the cosmic immensity.  Each jump through hyperspace was a gamble, a leap of faith into the uncharted reaches of the universe.  The lure of the Xylos system, rumored to hold the key to faster-than-light travel, outweighed the risks, driving her and her crew ever closer to the edge of known space.
