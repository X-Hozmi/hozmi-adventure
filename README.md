
Sir Reginald, his worn leather jerkin creaking with every stride, spurred his mount onward.  The dusty track wound relentlessly through the unforgiving landscape, the sun beating down on his already parched throat.  He’d traded the relative safety of the King's court for the whispered promise of El Dorado, a city of gold said to lie hidden amongst the treacherous peaks.  Bandits, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation were mere inconveniences compared to the legendary wealth that awaited, or so the tattered map promised.  Each creak of the saddle, each labored breath, fueled his relentless pursuit.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova navigated the shimmering nebula, her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, a tiny speck against the immensity of space.  The jump to Kepler-186f had been risky, pushing the ship's ancient hyperdrive to its limits.  Ahead lay the possibility of discovering a new habitable world, a chance to escape the suffocating overpopulation of Earth.  But the void was a cruel mistress,  and the whispers of rogue gravitational anomalies and pirate fleets made the journey a constant gamble, a thrilling dance between exploration and survival in the breathtaking, terrifying expanse of the cosmos.
