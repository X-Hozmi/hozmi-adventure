
Sir Reginald, his armor gleaming faintly under the bruised twilight sky, spurred his destrier onward.  The rough track, barely a path in places, wound through a forest haunted by whispers of brigands and wilder things.  Each crest of the hill offered a breathtaking, if perilous, vista – rolling hills giving way to the distant, snow-capped peaks of the Dragon's Tooth mountains, their jagged silhouette promising further challenges.  His quest, to recover the stolen relic of Saint Elara, drove him relentlessly forward, the thrill of the unknown battling the constant gnawing fear of ambush.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  The ship shuddered slightly as they navigated the unstable wormhole, a shortcut across the vast gulf separating the Andromeda galaxy from their home in the Milky Way.  Her mission, to locate a lost colony rumored to possess advanced technology capable of saving their dying world, was fraught with the dangers of unpredictable spatial currents and the ever-present threat of pirates operating in the lawless fringes of known space.  The vastness of the cosmos, both beautiful and terrifying, filled her with a sense of awe and a profound loneliness.
