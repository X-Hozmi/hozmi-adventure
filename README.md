
The year is 1347.  A chill wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his horse onward.  The scent of woodsmoke and damp earth clung to the air, a stark contrast to the rumors of exotic spices and silks that fueled his journey eastward.  Bandits, treacherous passes, and the ever-present threat of disease were merely inconveniences in his quest to reach the fabled city of Samarkand, a gamble fueled by whispers of fortune and a thirst for the unknown that burned brighter than any campfire.  Each sunrise brought a new challenge, each sunset a renewed determination to persevere.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the starship *Stardust* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  The vast expanse of space, a tapestry woven with starlight and cosmic dust, dwarfed even her seasoned experience.  Their mission: to chart unexplored galaxies in search of habitable planets, a journey fraught with the dangers of asteroid fields, unpredictable wormholes, and the ever-present threat of encountering unknown, potentially hostile civilizations.  Yet, the allure of discovery, the promise of new worlds and unimaginable wonders, spurred her crew onward, across the uncharted territories of the cosmos.
