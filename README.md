
The year is 1347.  Dust coated Sir Kaelan's chainmail as he urged his weary horse onward, the setting sun painting the endless plains of France a fiery orange.  His quest: to reach the mythical city of Ys, rumored to hold a cure for the Black Death ravaging his homeland.  Each day brought new challenges – bandits lurking in shadowed forests, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation.  Yet, the whisper of Ys, and the hope it offered, spurred him onward, a solitary figure against the vast, unforgiving landscape.


Centuries later, Captain Elara Reyes piloted the starship *Stardust*, its hyperdrive humming a song of unimaginable speed.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a potentially habitable exoplanet light-years from Earth.  The journey was not without peril; asteroid fields threatened to pulverize the vessel, and the vast emptiness of space pressed in, a constant reminder of their isolation. But Elara, and her crew of intrepid explorers, pressed on, driven by the possibility of discovering new life and expanding humanity's reach across the galaxy.
