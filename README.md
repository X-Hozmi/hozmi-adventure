
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Sir Gareth spurred his destrier onward, the rhythmic clop of hooves a counterpoint to the frantic beat of his heart. His quest: to reach the mythical city of Eldoria, rumored to hold a cure for the plague ravaging his homeland.  He travelled through dense forests haunted by brigands, across treacherous mountain passes blanketed in snow, and forded rivers swollen with spring melt. Each sunrise brought new dangers, new challenges, but the hope of Eldoria spurred him on.

Alternatively, in 2742, Captain Elara Vance piloted the 'Stardust Drifter' across the swirling nebula of Xylos, her course charted by the faint signals of a long-lost colony.  Their jump drive hummed, a constant reminder of the immense distances between star systems.  They braved asteroid fields the size of small planets, navigated treacherous gravitational anomalies, and battled space pirates whose ruthlessness was only matched by their advanced weaponry.  The promise of a new beginning, a chance to establish contact with a long-lost human settlement, propelled them further into the unforgiving vastness of space.
