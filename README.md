To travel beyond the familiar village gates was to embrace uncertainty, to pit oneself against the raw, unyielding forces of nature and the unpredictable whims of humanity.

Each dawn broke upon a new horizon, promising encounters with unfamiliar cultures in bustling market towns, the discovery of ancient ruins swallowed by wilderness, or the perilous crossing of swollen rivers.
