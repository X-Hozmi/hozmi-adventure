
Sir Reginald, his chainmail gleaming under the pale sun, spurred his destrier onward.  The rutted track snaked through a whispering forest, the air thick with the scent of pine and damp earth.  His quest: to reach the mythical city of Eldoria, rumored to hold the lost scriptures of the ancients.  Bandits, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation were but minor inconveniences in his determined pursuit of glory and knowledge, a journey measured not in miles but in the sweat of his brow and the courage in his heart.


Millennia later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a potentially habitable exoplanet light-years from Earth.  Navigating asteroid fields, dodging space pirates, and meticulously calibrating the hyperdrive were all in a day's work for a seasoned interstellar explorer.  The vastness of space, beautiful and terrifying in equal measure, presented a challenge far greater than any medieval knight could ever imagine, yet the pursuit of discovery burned just as brightly within her.
