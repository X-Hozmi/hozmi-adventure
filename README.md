
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onward.  Ahead lay the unknown – a rumored city of gold nestled deep within the treacherous Black Forest, a destination whispered about in hushed tones in taverns across the land.  Bandits, wild beasts, and the unforgiving landscape itself were his constant companions, each sunrise a gamble against starvation and death.  Yet, the glint of potential riches, the allure of the unexplored, spurred him onward, a testament to the enduring human spirit's quest for adventure.


Millennia later, Captain Eva Rostova gripped the controls of her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*,  its engines humming a low, resonant thrum as it navigated the nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to chart a new hyperspace route to the Andromeda galaxy, a journey fraught with the perils of unmapped cosmic currents and the ever-present threat of space pirates.  The vastness of the cosmos, both beautiful and terrifying, offered challenges far beyond any medieval knight could imagine, but the promise of discovery, of unlocking the secrets of the universe, fueled her unwavering determination.
