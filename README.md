
The year is 1347.  Dust billows behind the caravan as it lumbers across the sun-baked plains, its slow progress punctuated by the rhythmic clip-clop of hooves and the occasional hawk cry.  Sir Reginald, his armor gleaming dully under the harsh sun, scans the horizon, ever vigilant for bandits or worse.  Their journey to the fabled city of Zerzura, a mythical oasis whispered about in taverns, is fraught with peril. Every bend in the dusty track could hold a hidden danger – wild beasts, treacherous terrain, or the ever-present threat of human cruelty. The lure of riches and glory, however, spurs them onward, a beacon in the vast, uncertain landscape.


Centuries hence, the stars themselves are the highways.  Captain Eva Rostova pilots the 'Stardust Drifter,' her ship a sleek, obsidian needle piercing the inky void between galaxies.  The hum of the hyperdrive is a constant companion, a soothing counterpoint to the breathtaking spectacle of nebulae and distant star clusters.  Their mission: to locate the legendary Xylos system, rumored to hold the key to interstellar travel faster than light. The journey, measured not in days but in light years, demands unwavering courage and precise navigation.  Yet, the promise of unlocking the universe's greatest secrets fuels their relentless pursuit, defying the vast emptiness of space.
