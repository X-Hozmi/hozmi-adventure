
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's horse as she urged it onward, the setting sun painting the craggy mountains a fiery orange.  Her journey to the fabled city of Zerzura was fraught with peril; bandits lurked in shadowed forests, treacherous rivers threatened to swallow her whole, and the gnawing hunger in her belly was a constant companion.  Yet, the whispers of Zerzura's untold riches, its shimmering spires, spurred her on, each hardship only strengthening her resolve to reach the legendary oasis.

Captain Rexx slammed his fist on the console, his gaze fixed on the swirling nebula outside his starship's viewport.  The jump to Xylos was proving more turbulent than anticipated, the gravitational pull of the black hole near Cygnus X-1 threatening to tear their vessel apart.  But Xylos held the key to the Galactic Federation's survival – a rare mineral capable of powering faster-than-light travel.  The risk was immense, the reward potentially life-altering for an entire civilization, and Rexx, ever the seasoned explorer, wouldn't let a little cosmic turbulence deter him from his mission.
