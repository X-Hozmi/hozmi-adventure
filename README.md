
Sir Reginald, his chainmail gleaming under the relentless sun, spurred his destrier onward. The rough track, barely more than a goat path, wound through the unforgiving peaks of the Dragon's Tooth mountains.  His quest: to reach the fabled city of Eldoria, whispered to hold the lost scriptures of King Arthur.  Bandits, treacherous passes, and the ever-present threat of wild beasts were but minor inconveniences in his grand, perilous journey across the medieval landscape.  Each sunrise brought new challenges, each sunset the satisfaction of another league closer to his goal.


Thousands of light-years separated Captain Eva Rostova from her home planet, Xylos.  Her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, cut a solitary path through the swirling nebula of Xylos’s sister galaxy, Andromeda.  The mission: to locate and secure a rare element vital to Xylos' survival.  Warp jumps, asteroid fields, and the ever-present risk of encountering hostile alien lifeforms were all in a day's work for a seasoned explorer like Eva.  The vast emptiness of space held both unimaginable beauty and terrifying unknowns, a fitting backdrop to her intergalactic odyssey.
