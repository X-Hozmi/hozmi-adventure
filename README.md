
The year is 1347.  Dust choked the air as Sir Kaelan’s small band urged their weary mounts across the sun-baked plains.  Their quest, to reach the fabled city of Zerzura rumored to hold untold riches and ancient secrets, had already claimed several men to fever and bandit raids.  Each sunrise brought the promise of both breathtaking landscapes – soaring mountains and emerald oases – and the ever-present threat of ambush, starvation, and the unknown perils that lurked in the untamed wilderness.  Their journey was a tapestry woven with hardship, camaraderie, and the relentless pursuit of a dream whispered on the winds.


Centuries hence, Captain Elara Reyes piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: a daring expedition to chart the unexplored regions of the Andromeda galaxy, seeking out habitable planets for a desperate humanity fleeing a dying Earth.  Faster-than-light travel was a precarious dance with unstable wormholes and unpredictable cosmic anomalies, each jump a gamble against annihilation.  The crew, a motley collection of scientists, engineers, and hardened explorers, faced not only the vastness of space but the internal pressures of their desperate mission, testing their bonds of loyalty against the isolating expanse of the cosmos.
