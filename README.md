
The year is 1347.  Dust coated Elara’s boots as she spurred her destrier onward, the relentless sun beating down on the parched landscape.  Her journey, a pilgrimage to a rumored shrine possessing miraculous healing powers, had already taken her through bandit-infested forests, across treacherous mountain passes, and over rivers swollen with spring thaw. Each sunrise brought new challenges – unpredictable weather, the constant threat of wild animals, and the ever-present suspicion of strangers. Yet, the unwavering hope for her ailing brother fueled her onward, the vast, unknown expanse before her a tapestry woven with both peril and promise.


Centuries later, Captain Jaxx navigated the shimmering nebula of Xylos, his starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, a tiny speck against the breathtaking cosmic canvas.  The voyage to the Andromeda galaxy was fraught with perils: treacherous asteroid fields, unpredictable wormhole currents, and the constant hum of the ship’s aging engines.  But the lure of the legendary Xylos artifact, said to hold the key to faster-than-light travel, overshadowed all risks.  Jaxx, a seasoned explorer hardened by countless missions, gripped the controls, his gaze fixed on the swirling colours of the nebula, the boundless expanse of space his constant companion.
