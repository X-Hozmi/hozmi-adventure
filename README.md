
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's horse as she urged it onward, the setting sun painting the rugged landscape in fiery hues.  Her journey across the Holy Roman Empire was far from the courtly romances of song; it was a grueling test of endurance.  Bandits lurked in shadowed forests, rivers swelled with unpredictable fury, and the ever-present threat of disease hung heavy in the air.  Yet, with each sunrise, Elara’s determination burned brighter, fueled by the whispered legends of the lost city of Eldoria and the ancient power said to reside within its walls.  She carried a worn map, a compass, and a heart full of hope – the essential tools for a medieval adventurer.


Millennia later, Captain Rex steered the *Stardust Drifter* through a nebula of iridescent gas, its colours a breathtaking distraction from the daunting task ahead.  Their mission: to reach the Xylos system, a newly discovered cluster rumored to hold the key to faster-than-light travel.  Navigating treacherous asteroid fields, dodging space pirates, and battling the ever-present threat of temporal distortions, were all in a day’s work.  But the thrill of exploration, the promise of unlocking the universe’s secrets, and the camaraderie of his crew were enough to propel Rex and his ship across the vast expanse of space, a testament to human ambition in the far future.
