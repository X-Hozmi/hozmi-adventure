
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the plains as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his tired mare onward.  Ahead, the jagged peaks of the Dragon's Teeth mountains promised both peril and the rumored lost city of Eldoria, a place whispered to hold unimaginable riches.  Each creak of the saddle, each rustle in the tall grass, held the potential for bandits, wild beasts, or worse – the wrath of nature itself.  His journey was fraught with hardship, but the lure of adventure, the whisper of legend, fueled his weary steps.


Millennia later, Captain Elara Jax navigated the iridescent nebula of Xylos, her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, a tiny speck against the cosmic tapestry.  Her mission: to retrieve a vital data core from the abandoned research station on Kepler-186f, a planet orbiting a red dwarf star light-years from Earth.  The dangers were different now – hostile alien lifeforms, rogue asteroids, and the ever-present threat of equipment malfunction – but the thrill of exploration, the uncharted expanse of space, held the same intoxicating allure as the medieval adventurer's quest for lost cities.
