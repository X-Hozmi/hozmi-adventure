
In a future where humanity has shattered the bonds of Earth, adventure unfurls across the unimaginable canvas of the cosmos. Pioneers now chart courses through asteroid fields, slingshot around gas giants, and set coordinates for distant exoplanets orbiting alien suns. This is an era of interstellar exploration, driven by the insatiable human curiosity to discover what lies beyond the known, to seek out new civilizations, and to harvest the untold resources scattered across the galaxy. Every jump through hyperspace or wormhole is a leap into the unknown, a journey fraught with potential wonders and unforeseen perils in the silent, star-dusted void.
