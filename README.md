
Sir Reginald, his surcoat stained with mud and sweat, urged his weary steed onward.  The King's ransom, a chest overflowing with gold and jewels, was strapped to the pack horse behind.  Their journey across the treacherous Blackwood Forest had been fraught with peril – ambushes by brigands, treacherous bogs, and the ever-present threat of wild wolves.  Each day brought new challenges, but the lure of adventure, and the promise of riches beyond imagining, kept him pushing forward, towards the distant city of Porthaven and a future beyond his wildest dreams.

Millennia later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the starship *Stardust* through the swirling nebula of Xylos, its vibrant colours a stark contrast to the cold, calculated efficiency of the bridge.  Her mission: to locate the lost colony of Kepler-186f, a world rumored to hold the key to faster-than-light travel.  The vast expanse of space, littered with the remnants of ancient civilizations and the potential dangers of uncharted systems, held its own unique brand of terror and wonder.  Yet, the promise of unlocking the galaxy's deepest secrets, and perhaps even encountering new life, propelled her forward on her perilous journey.
