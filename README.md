
The year is 1347.  Dust billows behind Elara's worn boots as she trudges across the sun-baked plains, her hand perpetually gripping the worn leather hilt of her father's dagger.  The journey to the fabled city of Zerzura is fraught with peril – bandits lurk in the shadows of ancient ruins, and the whispers of desert spirits chill the night air.  Each sunrise brings a new challenge: navigating treacherous canyons, bartering with wary nomads, and enduring the relentless heat that threatens to break her spirit as surely as the sun cracks the parched earth.  Yet, the lure of Zerzura, a city said to hold untold riches and ancient secrets, fuels her onward.

Conversely, Captain Rexa "Nova" Valerius piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the iridescent nebula of Xylos, its swirling colours a stark contrast to the cold, calculated precision of her navigation.  This was no medieval quest; this was a galactic expedition, charting unmapped territories and seeking out new habitable planets for a dying Earth.  Each jump through hyperspace held the potential for both wonder and annihilation - rogue asteroids, gravitational anomalies, and the ever-present threat of pirate raiders.  Yet,  the promise of discovery, of finding a new home amongst the stars, outweighed the risk a thousandfold.
