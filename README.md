Traveling across vast, untamed lands meant braving dense, whispering forests rumored to harbor bandits or mythical beasts, crossing treacherous mountain passes veiled in mist, and navigating desolate plains under indifferent skies. Every journey was an odyssey, a test of will and courage against nature's raw power and humanity's darker impulses, where a forgotten path could lead to ruin and a chance encounter could spark a legend.
