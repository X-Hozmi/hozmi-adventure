
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the plains as Sir Reginald, his worn leather jerkin flapping, spurred his destrier onward.  His quest: to reach the fabled city of Zerzura, rumored to hold a cure for the plague ravaging his homeland.  Days bled into weeks, each sunrise revealing a new challenge – treacherous mountain passes, thieving bandits, and the ever-present threat of starvation. Yet, the lure of Zerzura, and the hope it represented, propelled him forward, each hardship forging him into a tougher, more resolute knight.


Millennia later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to deliver vital life-sustaining minerals to the struggling colony on Kepler-186f.  Navigating asteroid fields and dodging pirate scavengers was routine, but the true peril lay in the unpredictable currents of hyperspace, where a single miscalculation could mean oblivion.  The vastness of the cosmos, both beautiful and terrifying, reminded Eva of the courage and resilience demanded of those who dared to explore the galaxy’s uncharted reaches.
