
The creaking of the wagon wheels was a constant companion, the rhythmic pulse mirroring the relentless beat of Sir Gareth's heart.  Each sunrise brought a new vista across the rugged terrain of the Holy Roman Empire – dense forests giving way to sun-baked plains, the occasional castle spire a distant promise of shelter and respite.  Bandits lurked in the shadows, storms threatened to engulf their caravan, and the ever-present threat of disease hung heavy in the air. Yet, the lure of the fabled city of Ys, whispered to hold untold riches and arcane knowledge, pushed him onward, a beacon against the encroaching darkness.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Faster-than-light travel blurred the lines between reality and hallucination, the cosmos a kaleidoscope of impossible colours and breathtaking celestial formations. Her mission: to locate the lost colony of Kepler-186f, a planet rumored to hold the key to interstellar peace.  But the vast emptiness of space was no less perilous than the medieval wilderness; rogue asteroids, hostile alien encounters, and the ever-present threat of equipment malfunction posed constant challenges on her journey to the far reaches of the galaxy.
