
Sir Reginald, his chainmail gleaming under the relentless sun, spurred his destrier onward.  The rutted track, barely a path through the dense Ardennes forest, snaked ahead, promising both peril and untold riches.  Whispers of a lost monastery, overflowing with ancient scriptures and untold gold, fueled his journey.  Bandits, wild beasts, and treacherous bogs were mere inconveniences compared to the glory that awaited – a glory he would claim, sword in hand, for his King and his own renown. Every creak of the saddle, every rustle in the undergrowth, heightened the thrill of the unknown.


A thousand lightyears from Earth, Captain Eva Rostova gripped the controls of the *Stardust Drifter*.  The swirling nebula outside her viewport pulsed with colours unseen on any world she'd ever visited.  Her mission: to chart the unexplored Xylos sector, a region rumored to hold the key to faster-than-light travel.  The risks were immense – unstable wormholes, rogue asteroids, and the ever-present threat of pirate raiders – but the potential rewards, for humanity and for herself, were even greater.  Each jump through hyperspace was a leap of faith, a gamble with the very fabric of spacetime.
