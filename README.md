
Sir Reginald, his chainmail shimmering under the relentless sun, spurred his destrier onward. The dusty track, barely a path, wound through a landscape as harsh as the whispers of bandits that haunted his dreams.  Each creak of his saddle, each rustle in the parched undergrowth, sent a jolt of adrenaline through him.  He carried the King's writ, a desperate plea for aid from a besieged castle far to the east, a journey fraught with peril, but one he must undertake. The promise of glory, or perhaps just survival, spurred him further into the unknown.


Millennia later, Captain Eva Rostova gripped the controls of the *Stardust Drifter*, her knuckles white against the polished metal.  The swirling nebula outside the viewport painted the cockpit in a kaleidoscope of impossible colours, a breathtaking vista that belied the treacherous jump through hyperspace they were undertaking.  Their destination: Xylos, a planet rumoured to hold the key to interstellar travel’s next great leap – a gamble with unimaginable rewards, and the very real risk of being lost amongst the cold indifference of the cosmos.
