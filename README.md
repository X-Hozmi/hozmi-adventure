
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his pack heavy with meager supplies, pressed onward. His quest: to reach the fabled city of Zerzura, whispered to lie hidden in the heart of the Sahara.  Each sunrise brought new dangers – bandits lurking in shadowed canyons, treacherous sandstorms that threatened to swallow him whole, and the ever-present gnawing fear of thirst. Yet, the lure of the unknown, the whispered promise of untold riches and forgotten knowledge, spurred him relentlessly across the unforgiving landscape.


Millennia later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the iridescent nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to chart a new hyperspace route to the Andromeda galaxy, a journey fraught with perils far exceeding any medieval traveler could imagine.  Navigating treacherous asteroid fields, evading the gravity wells of rogue planets, and contending with the unpredictable nature of warp drives presented challenges that tested the limits of both technology and human endurance. But the vastness of space, the possibility of discovering new worlds and civilizations, fueled her unwavering determination to reach the unknown reaches of the cosmos.
