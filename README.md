
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's horse as she traversed the unforgiving landscape, the setting sun painting the jagged peaks of the Pyrenees a fiery orange. Her journey to the mythical city of Ys was fraught with peril: bandits lurked in shadowed forests, treacherous mountain passes tested her endurance, and the whispers of dark magic clung to the ancient ruins she passed.  Each sunrise brought new challenges – starvation, exhaustion, and the constant threat of violence – but the allure of Ys, a city said to hold unimaginable treasures and secrets, fueled her onward.


Centuries later, Captain Rexx slammed the hyperdrive into overdrive, the shimmering nebula outside their ship a breathtaking kaleidoscope of colours.  Their mission: to reach the Xylos system, a newly discovered cluster rumored to hold a planet teeming with a rare element vital to galactic stability.  The journey was a marathon through uncharted space, dodging asteroid fields, navigating treacherous wormholes, and evading the watchful eyes of the Kryll pirates.  The stakes were high, the odds were stacked against them, but the promise of scientific discovery, and perhaps untold riches, pushed them relentlessly forward.
