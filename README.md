
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she crested the windswept pass, the jagged peaks of the Dragon's Teeth mountains looming before her.  Her worn leather satchel held little besides dried meats, a chipped waterskin, and the tattered map guiding her to the fabled city of Eldoria.  Each creak of the saddle, each rustle in the tall grass, held the potential for bandits, wild beasts, or worse – the spectral riders said to haunt these desolate heights.  Yet, the lure of Eldoria, a city whispered to hold ancient magic, propelled her onward, despite the gnawing fear and relentless sun.


Centuries hence, Captain Ryla's starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, sliced through the nebula's iridescent clouds.  Her destination: Xylos, a planet rumored to harbor the mythical Kryll crystals, capable of powering faster-than-light travel across the galaxy.  The journey was fraught with dangers - rogue asteroids, unpredictable wormholes, and the ever-present threat of pirate raiders.  But Ryla, a veteran of countless interstellar voyages, held fast to her course, driven by the promise of unlocking secrets that could reshape the very fabric of galactic civilization.
