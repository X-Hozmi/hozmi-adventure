
Sir Gareth, his worn leather jerkin creaking with every stride, spurred his mount onward.  The muddy track, barely a path through the dense Ardennes forest, offered little comfort.  Days blurred into weeks, punctuated by skirmishes with bandits, desperate pleas for meager food and shelter from wary villagers, and the constant gnawing fear of the unknown.  Yet, with each sunrise, the allure of Camelot's beckoning banner—and the promise of the King's favor for his daring quest—propelled him forward, a lone knight against the vast wilderness.


A thousand light-years from Earth, Captain Eva Rostova navigated the shimmering nebula, her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, a tiny speck against the cosmic grandeur.  The jump to the Xylos system had been treacherous, the warp drive pushing the vessel to its limits.  Ahead lay the unexplored planet Kepler-186f, rumored to hold the key to faster-than-light travel, a discovery that could change the fate of the human race.  But the galaxy held its secrets close, and unknown dangers lurked in the shadowed corners of space, threatening to end their mission before it even began.
