
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his pack heavy with meager supplies, urged his weary horse onward.  The King's decree had sent him on a perilous quest – to find the legendary Healer's Herb, rumored to grow only in the shadowed valleys of the Dragon's Teeth mountains.  Bandit patrols, treacherous terrain, and the ever-present threat of starvation were but a few of the trials awaiting him on this arduous journey across the unforgiving landscape. Each sunrise brought new challenges, each sunset a prayer for survival.


Centuries later,  Captain Eva Rostova steered the starship *Starseeker* through a nebula of swirling crimson and gold.  Her mission: to chart the unexplored K'tharr system, a cluster of planets rumored to hold the key to faster-than-light travel.  The vastness of space, fraught with unpredictable solar flares and the ever-present threat of pirate raids, was a different kind of wilderness, demanding a different set of skills and a similar unwavering resolve.  The potential rewards, however, were far grander than any herb.
