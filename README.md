
Sir Reginald, his worn leather jerkin creaking with every stride, spurred his destrier onward.  The muddy track, barely a path through the dense Ardennes forest, offered little comfort, but the thrill of the unknown spurred him on.  He carried a king's ransom in silks and spices, a prize snatched from a treacherous Saracen caravan, and the whispers of a hidden monastery, rumored to hold the key to untold power, fueled his journey.  Wolves howled in the distance, a constant reminder of the perils that lurked beyond the flickering torchlight.

Conversely, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a potentially habitable exoplanet light-years from Earth.  The hum of the hyperdrive was a comforting counterpoint to the vast, indifferent expanse of space.  Asteroid fields, rogue gravitational pulls, and the ever-present threat of pirate vessels were commonplace hazards, but the potential discovery of new life, new technologies, and the expansion of human civilization made the risks worthwhile.  The galaxy was her playground, and the stars, her guide.
