
Sir Gareth, his worn leather jerkin creaking with each stride, spurred his destrier onward.  The sun beat down on the dusty track, the scent of thyme and sweat heavy in the air.  Ahead lay the Whispering Mountains, a legendary barrier rumored to guard a lost city of gold.  Each day brought new challenges – treacherous ravines, skirmishes with bandits, and the constant gnawing hunger. But the lure of adventure, the whispered tales of untold riches and glory, kept him pressing forward, his heart filled with a mixture of trepidation and exhilarating hope.


Millennia later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Starseeker* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  The jump to the Andromeda galaxy had been risky, pushing the ship's hyperdrive to its limits, but the potential reward – a planet teeming with a previously unknown energy source – was too enticing to resist.  Navigating asteroid fields and evading pirate patrols were commonplace hazards, and the vast emptiness of space, while beautiful, was also a constant reminder of their isolation.  But Eva, a seasoned explorer with a thirst for the unknown, pressed on, guided by the faint signal emanating from a distant world.
