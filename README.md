
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onward.  He clutched the worn map, its parchment brittle with age, showing the route to the fabled city of Zerzura.  Bandits, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation were constant companions on this perilous journey across the vast, unknown territories of the Holy Roman Empire. Each sunrise brought new challenges, each sunset a desperate hope that tomorrow would bring them closer to their goal.


Centuries later,  Captain Elara Reyes piloted her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  The jump to the Andromeda galaxy had been fraught with risk – unpredictable gravitational currents and the ever-present threat of pirate vessels roaming the void.  But the promise of the legendary Xylos crystal, capable of harnessing unimaginable energy, fueled her crew's unwavering determination.  Ahead lay uncharted space, teeming with potential riches and unknown dangers; a journey across the cosmos, as daunting and thrilling as any medieval pilgrimage.
