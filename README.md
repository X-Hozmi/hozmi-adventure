
The year is 1347.  Sir Reginald, his surcoat stained with mud and sweat, spurred his destrier onward.  The rough track, barely a path through the dense Black Forest, offered little respite from the relentless sun.  Each bend in the trail held the promise of bandits, wild animals, or worse – the rumored werewolves that haunted these shadowed woods.  His quest, to deliver a vital message to the King, was fraught with peril, every league a victory hard-won against the harsh realities of medieval travel. He carried the weight of his King's trust, and the fate of a kingdom, on his weary shoulders.


Thousands of years hence, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Wanderer* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Navigation charts flickered on her holographic console, displaying the treacherous asteroid fields ahead.  Faster-than-light travel was a marvel, but the cosmos remained a dangerous place.  Pirate fleets, rogue AI, and the unpredictable nature of wormholes all presented threats to her mission: transporting a vital cargo of xenoplants to the distant colony of Kepler-186f.  The vastness of space offered breathtaking beauty, but survival demanded constant vigilance and unwavering courage.
