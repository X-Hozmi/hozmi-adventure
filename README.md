Leaving the safety of one's village or castle, a traveler faced not just the vastness of unmapped territories, but a gauntlet of immediate threats. Dense, ancient forests held not only wild beasts but also outlaws lying in wait; treacherous mountain passes could collapse without warning; and disease was a constant, unseen companion on any long journey.
