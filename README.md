
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the plains as Sir Reginald, his worn surcoat flapping, urged his destrier onward.  Dust devils danced on the horizon, mirroring the turmoil in his own heart.  He was bound for the mythical city of Eldoria, whispered to hold the cure for the plague ravaging his homeland.  The journey was perilous; bandits lurked in shadowed forests, and treacherous mountain passes threatened to become his tomb. Yet, the hope of salvation, fueled by the whispered tales of Eldoria's wonders, spurred him on through sun-scorched days and starless nights.


Centuries later, Captain Elara Jax navigated the shimmering nebula of Xylos, her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, cutting through the cosmic dust.  Her mission: to locate the lost colony of Kepler-186f, stranded after a catastrophic wormhole incident.  The galaxy stretched before her, a breathtaking expanse of celestial bodies and hidden dangers – rogue asteroids, unpredictable gravitational fields, and the ever-present threat of pirate scavengers. But the possibility of finding survivors, of bringing them home, was a beacon brighter than any star, propelling her across the uncharted void.
