
Sir Reginald, his chainmail shimmering under the midday sun, spurred his destrier onward.  The dusty track wound through a seemingly endless expanse of rolling hills, the scent of pine and woodsmoke a meager comfort against the gnawing fear of bandits and the unknown.  Each creaking wagon wheel, each rustle in the undergrowth, sent a jolt of adrenaline through him.  His quest: to reach the fabled city of Eldoria, whispered to hold the key to ending a century-long drought, a journey fraught with peril, but one he was determined to complete.  He adjusted his grip on his sword, the thought of glory and the relief of his people spurring him onwards.


Thousands of light-years from Earth, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the 'Stardust Drifter' through the shimmering nebula of Xylos.  Navigation through the unstable wormhole had been treacherous, the ship buffeted by cosmic winds and gravitational anomalies.  Ahead lay Kepler-186f, a planet promising untold riches and scientific discoveries, but also rumored to harbor sentient life forms of unknown hostility.  The crew, a diverse band of explorers and scientists, held their breath, their hopes and fears reflected in the myriad stars swirling outside the viewport. The universe, vast and mysterious, held both wonder and danger in equal measure.
