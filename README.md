
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's horse as she urged it onward, the midday sun beating down on her worn leather jerkin.  Her journey across the sprawling plains of Francia was fraught with peril – bandits lurked in the shadows of ancient forests, and the ever-present threat of starvation clung to her like a shroud.  Yet, the whispered promise of a lost city, brimming with forgotten magic, spurred her onwards. Each creak of her saddle, each rustle in the tall grass, was a new challenge, a new test of her courage and resilience in this unforgiving land.

Centuries later, Captain Rexx navigated the swirling nebula, the crimson glow painting the bridge of the 'Stardust Drifter' in an eerie light.  His mission: to chart the unexplored sectors of the Andromeda galaxy, a voyage that would take generations.  The vastness of space held both wonder and dread;  the shimmering beauty of distant planets contrasted sharply with the ever-present risk of encountering hostile alien life or encountering unpredictable cosmic phenomena.  Yet, the allure of discovery, of pushing humanity's boundaries beyond the known universe, fueled his relentless pursuit across the star-strewn canvas.
