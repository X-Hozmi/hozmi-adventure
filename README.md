
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's worn boots as she crested the hill, the wind whipping her auburn braid across her face.  Before her stretched the endless expanse of the French countryside, a tapestry woven with sun-drenched fields and dark, foreboding forests.  Her journey to Avignon, to find a rumored cure for her ailing brother, was fraught with peril – bandits lurking in shadowed valleys, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of disease. Yet, with every sunrise, her determination burned brighter, fueled by the hope that lay beyond the horizon.


Centuries later, Captain Rexx slammed his fist on the console, cursing a rogue asteroid field.  His starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, bucked and swayed as it navigated the treacherous cosmic debris.  Their mission – to deliver a vital shipment of Xylos crystals across the Andromeda Galaxy – was a perilous one, demanding precision navigation and nerves of steel.  The vastness of space, both beautiful and terrifying, stretched endlessly before them, a constant reminder of the insignificance of man in the face of the universe, and the immeasurable risk they took for every precious cargo run.
