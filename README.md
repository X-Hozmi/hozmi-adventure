
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the exposed plains as Sir Reginald, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his weary steed onward.  Ahead lay the treacherous Black Mire, a whispered legend among travelers, and beyond that, the rumored lost city of Eldoria.  His quest, a perilous journey fueled by both ambition and the desperate need to secure a cure for his ailing sister, would test his courage and resilience against bandits, treacherous terrain, and the ever-present threat of starvation.  Each sunrise painted the sky in hues of hope and despair, mirroring his own fluctuating fortunes on this unforgiving pilgrimage.


Centuries hence, Captain Eva Rostova navigated the shimmering nebula of Xylos, her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, a tiny speck against the colossal backdrop of a thousand suns.  Her mission: to locate the fabled Xylos crystals, said to hold the key to faster-than-light travel.  The journey was fraught with dangers, from rogue asteroid fields to the unpredictable gravitational forces of dying stars, and the ever-present threat of the Kryll, a parasitic species that preyed upon interstellar travelers.  But Eva, a seasoned explorer with a thirst for the unknown, pressed on, her determination as bright and unwavering as the distant stars that guided her way.
