
The year is 1347.  Sir Kaelan, his worn surcoat flapping in the wind, spurred his destrier onward.  The trail, barely more than a goat track, snaked through the unforgiving peaks of the Dragon's Teeth mountains.  His quest: to reach the fabled Sunstone city, rumored to hold the cure for the plague ravaging his homeland.  Each day brought peril – bandits lurking in shadowed valleys, treacherous terrain, and the ever-present threat of starvation. Yet, the lure of the Sunstone, and the hope it offered, fueled his relentless journey across the harsh, beautiful landscape.


Millennia later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to locate the lost colony of Kepler-186f, a planet swallowed by a rogue wormhole decades ago.  Navigating through uncharted star systems, dodging asteroid fields, and battling the occasional space pirate, Eva relied on her wits and advanced technology to reach her destination. The vastness of space held both unimaginable beauty and deadly dangers, a constant reminder of the fragility of life amidst the cosmic expanse.
