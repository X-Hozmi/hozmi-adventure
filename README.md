
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she crested the pass, the jagged peaks of the Dragon's Teeth mountains looming before her.  Her journey to the fabled city of Zerzura, rumored to hold ancient secrets and untold riches, had been fraught with peril.  Bandits lurked in the shadowed forests,  storms threatened to sweep her from the treacherous mountain trails, and the ever-present hunger gnawed at her belly. Yet, the whispered promise of Zerzura, a beacon in the vast, unforgiving landscape, propelled her onward.

Centuries later, Captain Ryla's starship *Stardust Drifter* pierced the nebula, its engines humming a low, powerful song.  Navigation through the Xylos Cluster, a swirling maelstrom of gravitational anomalies and pirate activity, demanded absolute precision and nerves of steel. The bounty – a cache of Xylos crystals coveted across the galaxy – justified the risk.  Ahead, the shimmering emerald light of Kepler-186f beckoned, its alien landscape a tantalizing, and potentially dangerous, prize.
