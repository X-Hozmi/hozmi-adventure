
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his weary mule onward.  The scent of woodsmoke and damp earth clung to the air, a stark contrast to the tales whispered in taverns – tales of fabled cities shimmering with gold, and monstrous beasts guarding hidden treasures.  His quest for the lost monastery of St. Jude had led him through treacherous mountain passes, across sprawling plains haunted by brigands, and into the heart of a whispering forest where the very trees seemed to watch his every move. Each sunrise brought new challenges, each sunset a precarious victory in his perilous journey.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova’s starship, the *Starseeker*, punched through the nebula’s iridescent veil.  Her mission: to locate the rumored Xylos system, a cluster of planets said to hold the key to faster-than-light travel.  Navigating treacherous asteroid fields and evading the predatory K'tharr cruisers was child's play compared to the bureaucratic hurdles and political intrigue that threatened her mission.  But the lure of unexplored galaxies, the promise of discovering new life and unlocking the universe's secrets, propelled her forward, a lone voyager charting a course through the inky expanse of space, her heart as boundless as the cosmos itself.
