
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onward.  Ahead, the towering peaks of the Dragon's Teeth mountains loomed, a formidable barrier on his journey to the fabled city of Zerzura.  Bandits, treacherous passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation were his constant companions, yet the lure of untold riches and the whispers of ancient magic fueled his relentless pursuit. Each sunrise brought a new challenge, each sunset a hard-won victory in this perilous quest across the medieval landscape.

Conversely, centuries hence, Captain Eva Rostova piloted her starship, the *Starseeker*, through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a potentially habitable exoplanet light years from Earth.  Navigation through unpredictable gravitational fields and the ever-present threat of pirate attacks was a routine part of her life, yet the thrill of exploration and the hope of discovering new life forms surpassed any danger.  Each jump to hyperspace was a leap of faith, each planetary scan a breath held until confirmation of a new world – a frontier far removed from the mundane, a galaxy ripe for the taking.
