
The year is 1347.  A chill wind whipped across the rugged terrain as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his weary mount onward.  The King's ransom, a heavy chest strapped to the packhorse behind, bounced precariously with each jarring step.  Their journey across the treacherous Spine of the World mountains, a desperate gamble to reach the distant port of Genoa before the plague took them, was a brutal test of endurance. Every rustle in the undergrowth, every hawk circling overhead, sent shivers down his spine, reminding him of the dangers lurking in this unforgiving land – bandits, wild animals, and the ever-present threat of starvation.


Centuries later, Captain Elara Jax navigated the shimmering nebula, her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, a tiny speck against the cosmic canvas.  The jump to Xylos, a planet rumored to hold the key to interstellar travel, was fraught with peril.  Uncharted wormholes, unpredictable solar flares, and the ever-present threat of pirate raiders made each light-year a gamble.  But the allure of unlocking the secrets of faster-than-light travel, the promise of charting unknown galaxies, propelled her forward, a solitary explorer in the vast emptiness of space.
