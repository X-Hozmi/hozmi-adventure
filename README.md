
The year is 1347.  A chill wind whips across the rugged plains as Sir Gareth, his worn surcoat flapping, guides his weary mount onward.  The King's ransom, a chest bound in iron, is strapped securely behind his saddle.  His journey, fraught with bandit ambushes, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of disease, takes him far from the familiar comforts of court.  Each sunrise brings new challenges, each sunset a precarious victory against the harsh realities of medieval travel; a test of strength, resilience, and unwavering loyalty.


Conversely, Captain Eva Rostova steered her starship, *The Icarus*, through the swirling nebula.  The year is 2742.  Thousands of light years from Earth, her mission was to chart unexplored sectors of the Andromeda galaxy.  The vastness of space held wonders beyond imagination: shimmering planetary rings, pulsars that painted the void in breathtaking colours, and the eerie silence punctuated only by the hum of the ship's engines.   But the journey wasn't without peril; rogue asteroids, hostile alien encounters, and the constant threat of system malfunctions demanded unwavering vigilance and a cool head under pressure.
