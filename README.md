
The year is 1347.  Dust swirled around Sir Reginald's worn boots as he spurred his destrier onward, the midday sun beating down on his chainmail.  His quest: to reach the mythical city of Eldoria, whispered to hold the cure for the plague ravaging his homeland.  The journey was fraught with peril – bandits lurking in shadowed forests, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation and disease. Yet, with each sunrise, the allure of Eldoria, and the hope it represented, spurred him further across the unforgiving landscape.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the starship *Stardust* through the swirling nebula of Xylos. Her mission: to locate a rumored lost colony on the far side of the Andromeda galaxy, a journey spanning light-years and demanding the ultimate in navigational skill and courage.  Cosmic radiation crackled against the hull as she charted a course through asteroid fields and navigated treacherous gravitational anomalies.  The vastness of space, both beautiful and terrifying, held the promise of discovery – or utter annihilation.
