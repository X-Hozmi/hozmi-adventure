
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's horse as she urged it onward, the midday sun beating down on her worn leather jerkin.  Her journey across the rugged terrain of France was perilous; bandits lurked in shadowed forests, rivers swelled with unpredictable fury, and the whispers of plague stalked the land. Yet, the allure of the rumored lost city of Ys, with its untold riches and ancient secrets, fueled her onward. Each creak of the saddle, each rustle in the undergrowth, was a potential threat or a tantalizing clue, making this arduous land journey a thrilling, unpredictable dance with fate.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted her starship, *Stardust*, through the shimmering nebula of Xylos.  Navigating the treacherous asteroid fields and dodging space pirates was a routine, yet always exhilarating, part of her life.  Her mission: to chart unexplored galaxies in search of habitable planets for a burgeoning human diaspora.  Each jump through hyperspace brought the promise of discovery – a new world teeming with alien life, breathtaking celestial phenomena, or the chilling emptiness of a dead star. The vast expanse of the cosmos was her battlefield and playground, offering a uniquely perilous, awe-inspiring adventure far beyond the limitations of any earthly journey.
