
Sir Gareth, his armor gleaming under the relentless sun, spurred his destrier onward. The journey across the breadth of Albion was proving arduous; bandits lurked in the shadowed forests, treacherous bogs threatened to swallow man and beast whole, and the endless, dusty track seemed to stretch forever.  Each sunrise brought the promise of new dangers, each sunset the aching reality of another day’s brutal progress. Yet, the whispered tales of the fabled Dragon's Tooth, a legendary sword hidden in the far north, fueled his relentless pursuit, driving him ever closer to his destiny.


Millennia later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted her starship, the *Stardust Wanderer*, through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Vast cosmic distances were no longer a barrier, but the treacherous currents and unpredictable asteroid fields presented their own unique challenges.  The crew faced dangers far beyond medieval bandits: rogue gravitational wells, sentient space pirates, and the ever-present threat of hull breaches in the unforgiving vacuum of space. Yet, the promise of a new, habitable world on the far side of the galaxy, a world teeming with untold wonders and resources, kept the *Stardust Wanderer* on its perilous course.
