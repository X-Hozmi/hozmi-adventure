
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the vast, muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his weary horse onward.  The journey from York to the Holy Land felt endless, each sunrise revealing another stretch of treacherous terrain: dense forests teeming with bandits, unforgiving mountains, and rivers swollen with spring melt.  Each night brought the gnawing fear of wild animals and the chilling uncertainty of finding safe shelter. Yet, the lure of Jerusalem, the whispered promise of redemption and adventure, propelled him forward, fueled by faith and a stubborn, unwavering spirit.


Thousands of years later, Captain Elara Reyes piloted her starship, the 'Stardust Drifter,' across the swirling nebula of Xylos.  The vastness of space, a breathtaking tapestry of colours and cosmic wonders, held both unparalleled beauty and terrifying unknowns.  Navigation was a constant battle against unpredictable gravity wells and rogue asteroids, each jump through hyperspace a gamble against the ever-present threat of spatial distortion.  But the quest for the legendary lost colony of Kepler-186f, a whispered legend among interstellar explorers, drove her onward, fueled by a thirst for discovery and the unwavering hope of finding something extraordinary amongst the stars.
