
The year is 1347.  Dust coated Elara's chapped lips as she urged her weary mule onward.  The sun beat down on the endless expanse of the Rhône valley, the distant Alps shimmering with a deceptive promise of cool air.  Her journey to Avignon, a pilgrimage fueled by desperate hope and a worn map promising a miracle cure for her ailing brother, was fraught with peril.  Bandits lurked in the shadows of ancient forests, and the whispers of the plague clung to the wind like a shroud. Each sunrise was a gamble, each sunset a prayer.

Centuries later, Captain Jax navigated the shimmering nebula of Xylos, her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, a speck against the cosmic canvas.  The jump to Kepler-186f had been smoother than anticipated, but the thrill of exploration never waned.  Ahead lay a planet teeming with unknown life, a chance to uncover new resources and unravel the mysteries of a galaxy brimming with wonders – and potential dangers. The vast emptiness of space held both infinite opportunity and the chilling possibility of the unknown.
