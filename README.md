
The year is 1347.  Sir Gareth, his worn surcoat flapping in the wind, spurred his destrier onward.  The rutted track wound through a landscape as unforgiving as the rumors that preceded him – bandits, blizzards, and the ever-present threat of starvation dogged his every step.  He carried a king's desperate plea, a letter sealed with molten gold, promising riches and glory to whoever delivered it across the treacherous Spine of the World to the besieged city of Eldoria.  His journey was a tapestry woven with the threads of hope, fear, and the unwavering spirit of a knight bound by his oath.

Fast forward millennia.  Captain Eva Rostova gripped the controls of the 'Stardust Drifter', her knuckles white against the polished metal.  The hyperdrive hummed, a low thrum that vibrated through her very bones as they jumped between the swirling nebulae of the Andromeda Galaxy.  Her mission: to retrieve a vital bio-sample from the uncharted planet Xylos, a world rumored to hold the key to interstellar longevity.  The vastness of space, a breathtaking panorama of cosmic wonders, held both immense beauty and terrifying unknowns, a challenge that tested her courage and skill as she navigated the perilous currents of the universe.
