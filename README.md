
The creaking of the wagon wheels was a constant companion to Elara as she traversed the dusty medieval road, the scent of woodsmoke and sweat clinging to the air.  Each sunrise revealed a new landscape – rolling hills giving way to dense forests, then stark mountains piercing the sky.  Bandits were a constant threat, lurking in the shadows, but the allure of the fabled Sunstone city, whispered about in hushed tones in taverns, spurred her onward.  Every encounter, every hardship, fueled the growing legend of her journey.


Thousands of lightyears separated Jax from his homeworld, Xylos.  The hum of the Althea's hyperdrive was a lullaby against the endless void of space, punctuated by the crackle of comms as Jax navigated the treacherous asteroid fields and charted a course through unmapped nebulae.  His mission: to locate the lost colony of Aethel, rumored to possess technology capable of saving Xylos from its dying sun. The vastness of space was both terrifying and awe-inspiring, each celestial body a silent testament to the universe's grand, indifferent beauty.
