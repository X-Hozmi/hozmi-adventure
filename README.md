
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's horse as she urged it onward, the midday sun beating down on her worn leather jerkin.  Her journey across the treacherous Dragon's Tooth mountains was fraught with peril – bandits lurked in shadowed passes, wild beasts roamed the unforgiving slopes, and the very air seemed to crackle with unseen dangers.  Yet, the whispered tales of the mythical Sunstone, said to grant immortality, spurred her on. Every creak of the saddle, every strained muscle, fueled her determination to reach the fabled monastery hidden amidst the peaks.

Centuries later, Captain Rexx slammed the hyperdrive lever forward, his starship *Stardust Drifter* leaping across the void between the Andromeda and Milky Way galaxies.  Navigating the treacherous asteroid fields and avoiding the clutches of the Kryll pirates was child's play compared to the bureaucratic nightmare of obtaining the intergalactic permits for this illicit mission.  His cargo? A single, iridescent feather, rumored to be from the mythical Phoenix bird of Xylos, a creature whose existence was questioned by most, but whose rumored regenerative properties were worth risking everything for.
