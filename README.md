
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's horse as she urged it onward, the setting sun painting the endless plains of France a fiery orange.  Her leather jerkin, stiff with dried mud and sweat, offered little protection from the biting wind. Ahead lay the rumored lost city of Ys, a fantastical goal whispered about in hushed tones in taverns across the land.  Each creak of the saddle, each rustle in the tall grass, held the promise of adventure – or peril.  Bandits, treacherous terrain, and the ever-present threat of starvation were constant companions on her perilous journey.


Centuries later, Captain Rexx slammed his fist on the control panel, the ship shuddering in response as they jumped across the void between galaxies.  The shimmering nebula outside their viewport was a breathtaking spectacle, a kaleidoscope of cosmic dust and swirling gases.  Their mission: to locate the mythical Xylos star system, said to hold the key to faster-than-light travel.  But the vastness of space held its own terrors - rogue asteroids, pirate fleets, and the unnerving silence of the infinite – all challenges to overcome in pursuit of their audacious goal.
