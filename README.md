
**Future Era: Galactic Expeditions**

In a future where humanity has mastered interstellar flight, adventure stretches beyond the confines of a single world, beckoning across the cosmic ocean. Brave pioneers pilot sleek starships through wormholes and hyperspace lanes, leaving behind the familiar glow of their home sun to chart courses to distant, unnamed nebulae and uncharted star systems. Each jump is a leap of faith into the unknown, driven by an insatiable curiosity to discover what wonders and mysteries lie hidden among the countless stars, from nascent star nurseries to the silent graveyards of dead suns.

These galactic explorers face perils far greater than any earthly journey: volatile stellar phenomena, encounters with ancient or unknown alien civilizations, the profound isolation of light-years, and the sheer unpredictability of cosmic frontiers.
