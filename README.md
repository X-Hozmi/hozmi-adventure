
The year is 1347.  Sir Reginald, his worn leather jerkin creaking with each stride, urged his weary mount onward.  The trail, a barely discernible track through the dense Black Forest, wound ever deeper into the unknown.  His quest: to reach the fabled city of Eldoria, rumored to hold the cure for the plague ravaging his homeland.  Each rustle in the undergrowth, each distant wolf howl, fueled a mixture of dread and thrilling anticipation as he ventured further into the heart of untamed wilderness, his loyal squire a silent shadow at his side.

Alternatively,  Captain Eva Rostova piloted the 'Stardust Drifter' through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a potential colony world light-years from her home in the Sol system.  The hum of the Alcubierre drive was a constant companion, a reminder of the immense distances conquered and the even greater ones still before them.  The vastness of space, speckled with distant suns and swirling cosmic dust, held both the promise of a new beginning and the ever-present threat of uncharted dangers – a rogue asteroid field, a hostile alien patrol, or the silent failure of a crucial system.
