
The year is 1347.  Sir Reginald, his worn leather jerkin clinging to his sweat-soaked skin, spurred his weary horse onward.  The dust of the King's Road swirled around them, a gritty testament to the weeks spent traversing the harsh landscape.  He'd faced bandits, treacherous mountain passes, and the gnawing hunger of the unforgiving wilderness, all in pursuit of the mythical Sunstone, rumored to hold unimaginable power.  Each sunrise brought renewed hope, each sunset a gnawing fear that the legend was just that – a legend.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a potential colony world light-years from Earth.  The journey was perilous; navigating asteroid fields, evading space pirates, and battling the ever-present threat of warp storms kept her crew on edge.  Yet, the promise of a new beginning, of charting unknown territories and establishing a foothold amongst the stars, fueled their relentless pursuit across the vast, uncharted cosmos.
