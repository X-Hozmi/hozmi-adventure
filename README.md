
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his weary horse onward.  Ahead, the looming shadow of the Dragon's Tooth mountains promised both peril and the rumored lost city of Eldoria, said to hold unimaginable riches.  Each creak of the saddle, each rustle in the overgrown fields, sent a shiver down his spine – the wildness of the land itself was a constant companion, a threat as real as any bandit or beast.  He carried only a worn map, a prayer, and the burning hope of fortune.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova gripped the controls of the *Stardust Drifter*, her knuckles white.  The swirling nebula outside the viewport pulsed with unimaginable colours, a breathtaking tapestry of gas and dust.  Their mission: to chart the unmapped Kepler-186f system, a journey fraught with the dangers of asteroid fields, unpredictable wormholes, and the ever-present threat of unknown alien life.  The vast expanse of space offered unimaginable wonders, but held the potential for destruction just as readily.  Her crew's survival, and the future of interstellar exploration, rested on her shoulders.
