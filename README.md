
The year is 1347.  Sir Reginald, his armor gleaming dully in the relentless sun, spurred his destrier onward.  Dust billowed behind them, a swirling brown cloud trailing the small caravan as it wound its way across the unforgiving landscape.  Their journey, a pilgrimage to the rumored shrine of Saint Elara, was fraught with peril.  Bandits lurked in the shadowed forests, the terrain itself threatened to swallow them whole, and the ever-present threat of disease hung heavy in the air.  Yet, the lure of the unknown, the whispered tales of miracles and ancient power, kept them pressing forward, their hearts pounding a rhythm against the relentless beat of their horses' hooves.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova gripped the controls of the *Starseeker*, her knuckles white.  The jump to the Xylos system was imminent, a risky hyperspace transit through a newly charted nebula.  Beyond lay the planet Xylos Prime, a world rumored to hold the key to faster-than-light travel – a prize worth risking the unknown dangers of uncharted space.  The hum of the ship's engines filled the bridge, a constant, low thrum that mirrored the nervous anticipation of her crew.  The vastness of space, both beautiful and terrifying, stretched before them, a canvas of shimmering stars and dark, unexplored voids.
