
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onwards.  He clutched the ancient map, its parchment brittle with age, detailing a rumored lost city nestled amidst the treacherous peaks of the Dragon's Tooth mountains.  Bandits, wild beasts, and the ever-present threat of starvation were his constant companions, but the lure of untold riches and forgotten magic fueled his perilous journey across the unforgiving landscape. Each creak of the saddle, each rustle in the undergrowth, held the promise of both danger and discovery.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova steered the *Stardust Drifter* through a nebula of iridescent dust.  Her destination: Xylos, a planet whispered to hold the key to faster-than-light travel.  The vastness of space, punctuated by the distant, cold gleam of stars, was both awe-inspiring and terrifying.  Navigating asteroid fields, evading space pirates, and dealing with the unpredictable nature of warp jumps were all part of the interstellar voyage.  The potential rewards, however, were immeasurable – the unlocking of a new era of galactic exploration.
