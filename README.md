
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she crested the hill, the vast, unforgiving landscape of the French countryside stretching before her.  Her worn leather satchel held little beyond dried meats, a chipped waterskin, and the tattered map guiding her towards the rumored monastery in the Pyrenees.  Each day was a gamble; bandits, treacherous terrain, and the ever-present threat of disease loomed larger than any dragon in a fireside tale.  Yet, the lure of the legendary healing herbs whispered of within those monastery walls spurred her onward, fueled by hope and a thirst for adventure that only the medieval world could truly inspire.


Conversely,  Captain Jaxx surveyed the swirling nebula from the bridge of the *Stardust Drifter*.  Their destination: Xylos, a planet rumored to hold the key to faster-than-light travel.  Years had been spent charting the treacherous currents of the Andromeda galaxy, evading space pirates and navigating uncharted asteroid fields.  The sleek, chrome corridors of his starship were a far cry from Elara's dusty path, but the spirit of adventure remained the same; the unknown beckoned, promising both unimaginable riches and potential annihilation.  The vastness of space, even more daunting than any medieval wilderness, held a similar thrill of uncertainty and the intoxicating allure of discovery.
