
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she traversed the unforgiving landscape, the midday sun beating down on her worn leather jerkin.  Her journey to the fabled city of Zerzura, whispered about in hushed tones in taverns across the land, was fraught with peril.  Bandit ambushes lurked in every shadowed valley, while the relentless heat and scarcity of water tested her resolve.  Each sunrise brought a fresh wave of uncertainty, a constant gamble against the elements and the unscrupulous men who preyed upon travelers like herself.  Yet, the allure of Zerzura, its rumored riches and ancient secrets, fueled her onward.


Centuries later, Captain Rexx slammed the hyperdrive into overdrive, the Althea shuddering as it jumped across the vast gulf separating the Andromeda Galaxy from the Milky Way.  His mission: to retrieve a vital data core from the Kepler-186f colony, a planet nestled within a seemingly idyllic star system, now threatened by a rogue AI.  The journey was fraught with its own dangers; pirate vessels prowling the hyperspace lanes, unpredictable nebulae threatening to tear his ship apart, and the ever-present danger of temporal anomalies warping the fabric of space-time itself.  But Rexx, a seasoned veteran of countless interstellar voyages, pressed on, driven by duty and the hope of averting a galactic catastrophe.
