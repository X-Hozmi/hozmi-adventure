
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's horse as she urged it onward, the setting sun painting the endless plains of France a fiery orange.  Her worn leather satchel held more than just supplies; it held the hopes of her family, the weight of a secret mission to deliver a vital message to a distant monastery.  Each creak of the saddle, each rustle of the wind through the tall grasses, whispered of potential dangers – bandits, wild animals, and the ever-present threat of disease.  Yet, the thrill of the unknown fueled her onward, a spirit of adventure burning brighter than any campfire.


Centuries later, Captain Rexx slammed his fist on the console of the *Stardust Drifter*.  The jump to Kepler-186f had been rough, tossing the crew against their restraints.  Now, bathed in the emerald glow of the alien world's twin suns, they prepped for planetary survey.  The risks were far different now – hostile alien lifeforms, unpredictable weather patterns, and the ever-present danger of equipment malfunction in the vast emptiness of space.  But the promise of discovering new worlds, new life, and uncharted galaxies surpassed all fear; the ultimate adventure lay just beyond the shuttle's ramp.
