The world beyond one's village was largely unknown, mapped only by rumor and ancient tales, making each distant horizon a gateway to the utterly unpredictable.

Yet, those who dared to venture forth were met with more than just hardship. They discovered bustling foreign cities, marvelled at ancient ruins, and encountered cultures vibrant and distinct from their own.
