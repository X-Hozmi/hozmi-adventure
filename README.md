
The year is 1347.  Sir Reginald, his chainmail gleaming under the unforgiving sun, spurred his destrier onward.  Dust swirled behind them, a testament to their arduous journey across the unforgiving landscape.  News of a mythical city, shimmering with gold and rumored to hold ancient magic, had lured him from his castle walls.  Each day brought new challenges – treacherous mountain passes, skirmishes with bandits, and the ever-present threat of starvation. But the lure of adventure, the whisper of the unknown, fueled his relentless pursuit.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the starship *Stardust*, a speck of defiance against the infinite canvas of space.  Her mission: to chart the unmapped reaches of the Andromeda galaxy, seeking habitable planets for humanity's expansion.  Warp jumps flung them across light-years, each one a gamble against the unpredictable nature of hyperspace.  Alien encounters, both wondrous and terrifying, were commonplace. The vastness of the cosmos presented challenges far exceeding any medieval knight's, but the spirit of exploration, the burning desire to discover, remained the same.
