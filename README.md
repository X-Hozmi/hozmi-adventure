
Sir Reginald, his chainmail gleaming under the midday sun, spurred his destrier onward.  The dusty track wound relentlessly through the unforgiving terrain, the scent of pine and damp earth a stark contrast to the fear gnawing at his gut.  He was bound for the mythical city of Eldoria, whispered to hold the cure for his sister's ailment, a journey fraught with peril.  Bandits, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of wild beasts were just some of the obstacles he faced in his desperate quest across the medieval kingdom.

Thousands of lightyears from Earth, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the 'Stardust Drifter' through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  The vibrant colours painted a breathtaking backdrop to their mission:  to locate the legendary lost colony of Kepler-186f.  Navigation through asteroid fields and the constant threat of pirate raids made each jump through hyperspace a gamble, but the potential discovery of a new, habitable world fuelled their determination.  The vastness of space, both beautiful and terrifying, held the promise of untold riches—and immeasurable risks.
