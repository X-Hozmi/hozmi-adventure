
Sir Reginald, his worn leather jerkin creaking with each stride, spurred his destrier onward.  The sun beat down on the dusty track, the scent of pine and woodsmoke a distant memory as he traversed the unforgiving plains.  His quest, to reach the mythical city of Eldoria, whispered of in hushed tones by traveling merchants, drove him relentlessly forward, despite the gnawing hunger and the ever-present threat of bandits and wild beasts.  Each sunrise brought fresh challenges, each sunset the gnawing uncertainty of his journey's end.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova guided her starship, the *Stardust Wanderer*, through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Navigating treacherous asteroid fields and dodging the gravity wells of unseen planets was a daily routine. Her mission, to locate a lost colony on the far side of the galaxy, pushed her and her crew to their limits.  The vast emptiness of space offered both breathtaking beauty and terrifying isolation, a constant reminder of the perilous journey across the cosmic ocean.
