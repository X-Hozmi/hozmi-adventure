
The year is 1347.  Dust coated the worn leather of Thomas's saddlebags as he urged his weary horse onward, the setting sun painting the unforgiving landscape in hues of blood orange and bruised purple.  His quest, to reach the mythical city of Zerzura whispered to exist beyond the shifting sands,  had led him through bandit-infested forests, across treacherous mountain passes, and into the silent, watchful gaze of the endless desert.  Each sunrise brought the gnawing fear of starvation and dehydration, but the lure of the legendary city, and the whispered tales of untold riches and wonders, fuelled his relentless journey.


Centuries later, Captain Elara Reyes piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos, a kaleidoscope of cosmic dust and incandescent gas.  Her mission: to locate the lost colony of Kepler-186f, a planet swallowed by a temporal anomaly decades prior.  Navigating treacherous asteroid fields and encountering unpredictable gravitational forces was commonplace; the true danger lay in the unknown –  alien lifeforms, derelict starships concealing deadly secrets, and the ever-present threat of the temporal distortions themselves.  The vastness of space held both unparalleled beauty and terrifying peril, demanding courage, skill, and unwavering determination.
