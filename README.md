
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his weary horse onward.  His quest: to reach the legendary city of Eldoria, rumored to hold a cure for his ailing sister.  Each sunrise brought new challenges – bandits lurking in shadowed forests, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation. Yet, the hope of Eldoria, a shimmering mirage on the horizon of his imagination, fuelled his relentless journey across the unforgiving landscape of medieval Europe.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the starship *Odyssey* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to establish a trade route between the Andromeda galaxy and the Sol Federation, a perilous voyage through uncharted space.  The dangers were different now –  unstable wormholes, hostile alien lifeforms, and the constant hum of the ship’s failing hyperdrive. But the promise of interstellar commerce, of uniting disparate civilizations, spurred her onward, the vast expanse of the cosmos her relentless, beautiful battlefield.
