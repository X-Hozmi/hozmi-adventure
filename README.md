
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onward.  His knapsack, heavy with meager supplies and a worn map promising riches in the distant kingdom of Eldoria, felt like a lead weight on his back.  Each creak of the cart, each rustle in the overgrown fields, held the promise of bandits, wild animals, or worse.  He pressed on, driven by a thirst for adventure and the hope of escaping the poverty of his village, a lone figure against the vast, uncertain landscape of medieval Europe.

Centuries later, Captain Elara Reyes piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  The vibrant colours painted across her viewport were breathtaking, but the mission was perilous: retrieving a vital energy source from the abandoned mining colony on Kepler-186f.  Navigating treacherous asteroid fields and evading the notorious space pirates of the Crimson Syndicate was all in a day's work for a seasoned interstellar explorer, but the sheer scale of the cosmos and the loneliness of deep space always held a certain thrilling dread. The allure of the unknown, however, far outweighed the risks.
