
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the plains as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his weary horse onward.  The King's decree had summoned him to the distant city of Prague, a journey fraught with peril. Bandits lurked in the shadowed forests, rivers swelled with the spring thaw, and rumors of a plague stalked the land. Each sunrise brought a new challenge, a new test of his endurance and cunning.  His pack held little more than dried meat, a worn map, and the hope of a safe arrival.


Centuries later, Captain Elara Reyes piloted the 'Stardust Drifter' through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a potentially habitable exoplanet light-years from Earth.  Navigating asteroid fields, dodging pirate raids from the Kryll Confederacy, and wrestling with the unpredictable quirks of faster-than-light travel were all in a day's work.  The vastness of space, both beautiful and terrifying, held the promise of discovery – but also the ever-present threat of the unknown.
