
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Sir Kaelan's weary steed as he traversed the unforgiving landscape of the Holy Roman Empire.  His quest, to deliver a vital message to the besieged city of Avignon, was a perilous one.  Bandits lurked in the shadowed forests, treacherous mountain passes threatened to send both man and beast tumbling to their doom, and the constant gnawing hunger was a foe as relentless as any human enemy. Each sunrise brought new challenges, each sunset the chilling reminder of his isolation amidst the vast, untamed wilderness.

Conversely, Captain Elara Jax navigated the shimmering nebula of Xylos in her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*.  Her mission: to locate the legendary Xylossian crystals, rumored to hold the key to faster-than-light travel.  Navigating asteroid fields, evading pirate vessels, and deciphering ancient alien maps were all part of the routine.  The vast emptiness of space, punctuated by the breathtaking beauty of distant galaxies, was both awe-inspiring and terrifying, a stark contrast to the earthy dangers faced by medieval travelers, yet just as demanding in its own right.
