
The year is 1347.  Sir Kaelan, his surcoat stained with mud and sweat, urged his weary steed onward.  The shimmering heat haze distorted the already indistinct horizon of the French countryside, a stark contrast to the biting winds they'd endured just days before in the Alps.  His quest: to deliver a vital message to the King, a journey fraught with peril from bandits, treacherous terrain, and the ever-present threat of disease.  Each sunrise brought new challenges – fording raging rivers, navigating dense forests, and bartering for meager sustenance in wary villages. This was the medieval age, where adventure wasn't a game, but a relentless fight for survival.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a potential colony world light-years from Earth.  Navigating asteroid fields and battling rogue subspace currents was commonplace; the true danger lay in the unknown.  Uncharted systems held untold wonders and unspeakable horrors, from sentient energy beings to derelict alien vessels containing forgotten technologies.   Eva, a seasoned explorer, relied on her wits, her trusty crew, and the advanced technology of her starship to overcome the immense challenges of interstellar travel in a galaxy teeming with both beauty and danger.
