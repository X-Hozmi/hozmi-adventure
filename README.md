
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the plains as Elara, her worn leather jerkin flapping, urged her horse onward.  Ahead lay the rumored city of Zerzura, a mythical oasis whispered about in hushed tones in taverns across the land.  Bandits, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation were but minor inconveniences compared to the allure of finding this lost paradise, a journey fueled by ancient maps and a heart brimming with reckless hope.  Each sunrise brought new challenges, each sunset a breathtaking vista across the unforgiving landscape.


Centuries later, Captain Ryla Kael navigated the shimmering nebula of Xylos, her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, a tiny speck against the backdrop of a million blazing suns.  The destination: Kepler-186f, a promising exoplanet potentially harboring life.  The journey across the vast gulf between stars, punctuated by warp jumps and the ever-present threat of rogue asteroids and unforeseen gravitational anomalies, demanded precision, courage, and unwavering focus.  But the potential discovery of a new civilization, and the expansion of humankind’s reach beyond its solar cradle, made every risk worthwhile.
