
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she crested the windswept hill, the vast expanse of the French countryside stretching before her.  Her worn map, more faded patches than ink, guided her towards the rumored monastery holding the lost Gospels.  Bandit raids and treacherous mountain passes were mere inconveniences compared to the thrill of the unknown, the allure of uncovering history itself. Each sunrise brought a new challenge – navigating unfamiliar dialects, bartering for supplies, and always, the ever-present fear of the wild.

Centuries later, Captain Rexx slammed his fist on the console, the crimson nebula swirling outside his starship's viewport.  Their hyperspace jump had been rough, throwing the crew of the *Stardust Drifter* against their restraints.  The Xylos system, a cluster of planets rumored to hold the legendary Element X, beckoned – a prize worth risking the jump's volatile energy signatures.  Ahead lay not just vast distances, but alien civilizations, hostile environments, and the ever-present threat of pirates, proving that the adventure of exploration, though vastly different in setting, remained timeless.
