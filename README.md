
The year is 1347.  Dust choked the air as Sir Kaelan’s retinue crested the pass, the unforgiving peaks of the Dragon’s Teeth range looming behind them.  Their journey to the Sultan’s court had been fraught with peril – bandits lurking in shadowed forests, treacherous river crossings, and the ever-present threat of starvation. Yet, the lure of the Sultan’s legendary jade dragon, whispered to possess untold power, spurred them onward. Each arduous mile was a testament to their courage, a step closer to fortune or ruin.


Centuries later, Captain Elara Jax navigated the shimmering nebula of Xylos, her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, a tiny speck against the grandeur of the cosmos.  The jump to the Kepler-186f system had been smoother than expected, but the unknown dangers of this newly charted planet loomed large.  Alien flora pulsed with bioluminescent light, and the sensors hinted at life forms both beautiful and potentially deadly.  The promise of a new world, teeming with untold resources and mysteries, fueled her relentless pursuit of exploration, a legacy echoing the intrepid adventurers of old.
