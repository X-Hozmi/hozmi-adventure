
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's worn boots as she urged her mule onward, the midday sun beating down on the sun-baked track.  Her journey to the fabled city of Zerzura, a shimmering mirage whispered about in taverns and hushed conversations, was fraught with peril.  Bandits lurked in the shadows of ancient ruins, and the unforgiving desert tested her endurance daily.  But the promise of untold riches and the thrill of the unknown fueled her onward, each sunrise a renewed pledge to conquer the vast and treacherous landscape.


Centuries later, Captain Rexx slammed his fist on the console, the jarring impact echoing through the bridge of the 'Stardust Drifter'.  Their jump to the Xylos system had been rough, a near-miss with a rogue asteroid sending a shiver down the spine of even the most seasoned crew.  Ahead lay the unexplored Xylos nebula, a swirling vortex of cosmic dust and untold wonders – and dangers. The allure of discovering new planets and alien civilizations, despite the inherent risks of navigating the uncharted depths of space, was a siren song no explorer could resist.
