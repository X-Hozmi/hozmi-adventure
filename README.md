
Sir Reginald, his chainmail shimmering under the relentless sun, spurred his destrier onward.  The dusty track snaked through the unforgiving landscape, a ribbon of ochre against the parched earth.  Ahead lay the rumored City of Whispers, a legendary metropolis said to hold untold riches and ancient secrets.  Bandits, treacherous terrain, and the ever-present threat of starvation were minor inconveniences compared to the glory awaiting him – if he could survive the perilous journey across the kingdom and beyond.

Decades later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Wanderer* through the iridescent nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to retrieve a vital bio-sample from the ice planet of Kepler-186f, a world light-years from her home in the Sol system. Navigating asteroid fields, evading pirate ships, and the ever-present threat of system malfunctions were all in a day's work for the seasoned explorer.  The riches she sought were not gold or jewels, but the key to interstellar peace and the survival of her people.
