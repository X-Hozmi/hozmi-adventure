
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's weary horse as she crested the hill, the vast, unforgiving landscape of the French countryside stretching before her.  Her worn leather satchel held little more than dried meat and a chipped map, but her heart held a fierce determination.  She journeyed to find her brother, lost amidst the chaos of the Hundred Years' War, navigating treacherous mountain passes, dodging bandit raids, and relying on the kindness – and occasional suspicion – of strangers in villages scattered across the land. Each sunrise brought fresh peril, each sunset a fragile hope of reunion.

Alternatively,  Captain Eva Rostova gripped the controls of the *Starseeker*, her knuckles white against the polished metal.  Outside, the swirling nebula of Xylos painted the viewport in a breathtaking kaleidoscope of colour, a stark contrast to the sterile hum of the ship's engine.  Her mission: to chart a new hyperspace route to the Andromeda galaxy, a journey fraught with unpredictable wormholes, hostile alien patrols, and the ever-present threat of spatial distortion.  The cosmos held untold wonders, but also unimaginable dangers, each jump a gamble with the very fabric of spacetime.
