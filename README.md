
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's horse as she urged it onward, the midday sun beating down on her worn leather jerkin.  Ahead lay the treacherous Whispering Mountains, a legendary pass rumored to be guarded by bandits and mythical creatures.  Her quest: to reach the fabled city of Eldoria, said to hold the cure for her ailing brother.  Each creak of the saddle, each rustle in the undergrowth, fueled her fear but also her determination;  this journey, though fraught with peril, held the promise of salvation.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova navigated the swirling nebula of Xylos, her starship, the *Stardust Drifter*, a tiny speck against the cosmic canvas.  The jump to Kepler-186f, a potentially habitable exoplanet, was risky, the hyperspace currents unpredictable.  But the promise of discovery, the chance to chart new territories for humanity, outweighed the danger.  The vastness of space, both terrifying and exhilarating, held a different kind of adventure, one measured not in leagues but in light-years.
