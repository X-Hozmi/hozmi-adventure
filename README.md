
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she crested the windswept hill, the spires of Carcassonne a distant shimmer in the afternoon sun.  Her worn leather satchel held more than just supplies; it contained the hope of finding her estranged brother, a rumour whispered in taverns of him joining a crusade to the Holy Land.  The journey was fraught with peril – bandits lurked in shadowed forests, rivers swelled with unpredictable fury, and the ever-present threat of disease hung heavy in the air. Each sunrise brought a new challenge, each sunset a renewed determination to press on.


Centuries later, Captain Ryla Jax navigated the Xylos Nebula in her starship, *The Wanderer*.  The jump to the Kepler-186f system had been harrowing, the gravitational currents nearly tearing her vessel apart. Now, she plotted a course through a swirling asteroid field, her sensors straining to detect any rogue space debris that could cripple her ship.  The rewards of this perilous intergalactic voyage – a rumored source of Xylium, the rarest and most powerful energy source in the known universe – were worth the risks, but even with advanced technology, the vastness of space and the unpredictable nature of celestial bodies presented constant threats.
