
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she traversed the unforgiving terrain of the Pyrenees Mountains.  Each creak of the saddle, each sharp intake of breath against the thin air, marked another step closer to the rumored lost city of Eldoria.  Bandits, starvation, and treacherous mountain passes were constant threats, yet the whispered tales of Eldoria's legendary treasures spurred her onward.  The scent of pine and the distant calls of eagles were a poor substitute for companionship, but the thrill of the unknown fueled her relentless journey.

Alternatively,  Captain Rexx "Comet" Valerius gripped the controls of the *Stardust Drifter*,  a battered but reliable freighter hurtling through the Andromeda Galaxy.  His mission: to deliver a shipment of rare Xylos crystals to the Kepler-186f colony, a risky endeavor fraught with pirate attacks and unpredictable nebulae.  The vast emptiness of space, punctuated by the shimmering light of distant stars and the hum of the ship's engines, was both awe-inspiring and terrifying.  The promise of a hefty payday and the thrill of exploring the uncharted reaches of the galaxy kept him pressing forward, even as the void whispered of countless unknown dangers.
