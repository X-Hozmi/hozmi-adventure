Whether a knight on a perilous quest for a mythical artifact, a merchant caravan braving bandit-ridden roads to distant markets, or a lone pilgrim seeking spiritual enlightenment at a faraway shrine, travel across the land was an undertaking fraught with both danger and discovery. Every winding forest path, every treacherous mountain pass, and every vast, open plain held the promise of untold riches, ancient secrets, or encounters with legendary beasts, transforming mundane journeys into epic sagas.

To embark on such an adventure was to embrace a world without maps, relying on folklore, unreliable guides, and the stars as compass.
