
The year is 1347.  Dust swirled around the caravan as it lumbered across the sun-baked plains, the rhythmic clang of the mule bells a constant companion to the creaking of wagon wheels.  Sir Gareth, his armor gleaming faintly under the harsh light, squinted at the horizon, his hand never far from the pommel of his sword.  Rumors of a lost city, overflowing with riches and ancient magic, fueled their perilous journey through bandit-infested territories and treacherous mountain passes.  Each sunrise brought a fresh wave of uncertainty, a constant battle against the elements and the ever-present threat of violence.


Centuries later, Captain Elara Reyes piloted the *Stardust Wanderer* through the swirling nebula of Xylos, her heart pounding with a different kind of fear.  The jump to the Andromeda galaxy had been risky, pushing the ship's hyperdrive to its absolute limits.  Ahead lay Kepler-186f, a planet rumored to hold the key to interstellar peace, but also guarded by formidable alien forces.  The vastness of space, a seemingly infinite expanse of stars and cosmic wonders, held both unimaginable promise and terrifying unknowns, a challenge far grander than any medieval knight could have ever imagined.
