
The year is 1347.  Dust billows behind Elara's mule as she crests a windswept hill, the spires of Carcassonne a distant promise.  Her journey across France, undertaken to deliver a vital message to her estranged brother, has been fraught with peril.  Bandits lurking in shadowed forests, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of sickness have tested her resilience.  Yet, the allure of reaching her destination, the hope of reconciliation, fuels her onward, each sunrise a renewed pledge to overcome the obstacles that lie ahead.  The vast unknown stretches before her, a tapestry woven with danger and anticipation.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova pilots the 'Stardust Drifter' through the shimmering nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to retrieve a vital energy source from a remote, uncharted planet. Navigating asteroid fields and evading the relentless patrols of the Kryll Empire demands unwavering focus and impeccable piloting skills.  The vast expanse of space, punctuated by breathtaking celestial wonders and lurking threats, demands courage and a relentless pursuit of the unknown. The galaxy unfolds before her, a canvas of limitless possibilities and unimaginable dangers.
