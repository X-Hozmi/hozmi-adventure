
Sir Reginald, his worn leather jerkin creaking with every stride, spurred his destrier onward.  The rutted track, barely a path through the dense Ardennes forest, offered little comfort.  Each bend held the promise of bandits, wild beasts, or worse – the chilling unknown that lay beyond the borders of his kingdom.  His quest: to reach the mythical city of Eldoria, rumored to hold the cure for his ailing queen.  He carried only his sword, a meager pouch of coins, and the unwavering hope that fuelled his perilous journey across the unforgiving landscape.

A thousand years hence, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the 'Stardust Drifter' through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a potentially habitable exoplanet light years from Earth.  Navigating asteroid fields and dodging the gravitational pull of rogue planets was a daily routine, but the thrill of discovery – the chance to uncover new life, new worlds – surpassed any fear.  The vastness of space, both beautiful and terrifying, was a constant reminder of the fragility of life, and the immense scale of the human endeavor to reach beyond the cradle of Earth.
