
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's horse as she rode, the relentless sun beating down on her leather jerkin.  Her journey across the kingdom was fraught with peril – bandits lurked in shadowed forests, treacherous mountain passes tested both beast and rider, and the ever-present threat of disease hung heavy in the air.  Yet, the whispered tales of a lost city, rumored to hold unimaginable riches and ancient secrets, spurred her onward. Each sunrise brought a new challenge, a new vista, a new chance to uncover the mysteries hidden within the untamed lands.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the starship *Stardust*, its sleek hull shimmering under the multi-hued nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to chart a new hyperspace route to the Andromeda galaxy, a journey fraught with the dangers of unmapped wormholes and hostile alien patrols.  The vastness of space, punctuated by breathtaking celestial wonders and the chilling emptiness between stars, dwarfed even the grandest medieval explorations. The reward, however, was the potential to unlock the secrets of the universe itself, a cosmic quest beyond the wildest dreams of any medieval adventurer.
