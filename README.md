
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's worn boots as she urged her mule onward, the setting sun painting the rugged landscape in fiery hues.  Her journey across the Breton countryside was fraught with peril – bandits lurked in shadowed forests, rivers swelled with unpredictable fury, and the ever-present threat of disease hung heavy in the air.  Yet, the whispers of a hidden monastery, rumored to hold the cure for her ailing brother, spurred her on. Each sunrise brought new challenges, each sunset a testament to her resilience, weaving a tapestry of hardship and hope across the medieval tapestry of her adventure.


Centuries later, Captain Ava Reyes piloted the 'Starseeker' through the swirling nebula of Xylos, its vibrant colors a stark contrast to the cold, calculated precision of her mission.  Her destination: Kepler-186f, a potentially habitable exoplanet light-years from Earth.  Navigating treacherous asteroid fields and contending with the unpredictable whims of hyperspace were mere inconveniences compared to the political intrigue and potential alien encounters that shadowed her journey.  The vastness of space, however, held no fear for Ava; the thrill of exploration burned brighter than any star.
