
The year is 1347.  Sir Reginald, his worn surcoat flapping in the wind, urged his destrier onward.  The trail, barely more than a rutted track, snaked through the dense Black Forest, promising both peril and untold riches.  Whispers of a lost monastery, brimming with ancient scriptures and untold gold, fuelled his relentless journey.  Each creak of the saddle, each rustle in the undergrowth, heightened the sense of adventure, a constant gamble against bandits, treacherous terrain, and the ever-present shadow of the unknown.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted her starship, the *Stardust Wanderer*, through the shimmering nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to locate the fabled Xylossian Crystals, rumored to hold the key to faster-than-light travel.  Navigating asteroid fields and dodging pirate vessels, she felt the thrill of exploration burn within her, a legacy of countless adventurers who had dared to chart the uncharted cosmos.  The vast expanse of space, both beautiful and terrifying, held the promise of unimaginable discovery—and the very real threat of annihilation.
