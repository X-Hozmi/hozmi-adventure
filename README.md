Bandits lurked in dense forests, wild beasts roamed forgotten paths, and treacherous weather could turn a simple journey into a desperate struggle for survival. Yet, across these perilous lands, the allure of discovery beckoned. Knights sought glory in distant lands, merchants plied their trade across kingdoms, pilgrims embarked on spiritual odysseys, and cartographers dared to chart the unknown, each journey a gamble promising either ruin or untold riches, new knowledge, or legendary tales.
