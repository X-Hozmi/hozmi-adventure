
Sir Reginald, his armour gleaming under the unforgiving midday sun, spurred his destrier onward.  The trail, little more than a rutted track, wound through a landscape both breathtaking and perilous.  Towering forests whispered secrets of ancient magic, while treacherous mountain passes offered glimpses of snow-capped peaks that scraped the heavens.  Each day brought new challenges – bandits lurking in shadowed glens, raging rivers to ford, and the constant gnawing hunger in his belly. Yet, the lure of the mythical Sunstone, said to grant unimaginable power, pushed him ever forward, a beacon in the vast, unknown wilderness.


A thousand light years from Earth, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the 'Starseeker' through the iridescent nebula of Xylos.  Navigating the treacherous asteroid fields was child's play compared to the political minefield she found herself in.  The galactic council's ban on Xylos's rare element, Xylium, threatened interstellar peace, and securing a shipment for her ailing home planet was a mission of paramount importance.  Each jump through hyperspace was a gamble, a leap of faith into the vast, uncharted ocean of the cosmos, where the dangers were as varied and unpredictable as the stars themselves.
