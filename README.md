
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she traversed the unforgiving landscape, the midday sun beating down on her worn leather jerkin.  Each creak of the saddle warned of the perilous journey ahead – bandits lurking in shadowed forests, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of starvation.  Her quest, to reach the legendary city of Eldoria and deliver a vital message, pushed her onward, fueled by a mixture of courage and desperation. The rough-hewn map clutched in her hand was her only guide through the vast, unknown expanse of the kingdom.


Centuries later, Captain Rexx slammed the hyperdrive lever forward, his ship, the 'Stardust Drifter,' leaping across the void between galaxies.  The shimmering nebula outside his viewport painted a breathtaking, yet alien, landscape. His mission, a daring exploration of the newly discovered Xylos system, promised untold riches and unimaginable dangers.  Navigating asteroid fields and evading the predatory K'tharr warships were merely a prelude to the mysteries that lay waiting on the uncharted planets below – a daunting, but exhilarating, prospect.
