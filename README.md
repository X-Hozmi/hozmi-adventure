
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the plains as Sir Gareth, his worn surcoat flapping, spurred his destrier onward.  His quest: to reach the fabled city of Zerzura, whispered to hold the cure for a plague sweeping his homeland.  Each day brought new perils – bandits lurking in shadowed forests, treacherous mountain passes choked with snow, and the ever-present threat of starvation and disease. Yet, the lure of Zerzura, and the hope it represented, propelled him forward, a lone knight against the vast, unforgiving landscape of medieval Europe.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Starseeker* through the iridescent nebula of Xylos. Her mission: to locate a lost colony on the far side of the galaxy, a signal detected only as a faint whisper across the cosmic void.  Navigating asteroid fields and dodging the gravitational pull of black holes was child's play compared to the political intrigue and potential sabotage plaguing her crew.  The journey, a breathtaking odyssey through uncharted space, tested her courage and skill as much as the vast, mysterious expanse between planets demanded.
