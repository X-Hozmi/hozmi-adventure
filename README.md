
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onward.  Ahead lay the treacherous Black Forest, whispered to be haunted by witches and wild beasts, but beyond that lay the fabled city of  Serinor, rumored to hold unimaginable riches and a cure for his ailing sister. Each creak of his saddle, each rustle in the undergrowth, fueled the thrill and terror of his adventure, a desperate gamble against the unforgiving landscape and the uncertain whispers of fate.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the starship *Stardust*, its sleek hull gleaming under the light of a nebula.  Her mission: to traverse the Andromeda galaxy, searching for a lost colony that vanished centuries ago.  The vast, inky blackness of space held both breathtaking beauty and terrifying unknown dangers – rogue asteroids, solar flares, and the ever-present threat of hostile alien encounters. Yet, the lure of discovery, the hope of finding her ancestors, propelled her forward across the unimaginable distances between worlds.
