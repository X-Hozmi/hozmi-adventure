
The year is 1347.  A chill wind whipped across the plains as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his weary horse onward.  His quest: to reach the fabled city of Zerzura, rumored to hold untold riches and ancient secrets.  Bandits, treacherous mountain passes, and the ever-present threat of disease were constant companions on his arduous journey, testing his courage and resilience at every turn. Each sunrise brought new challenges, each sunset a renewed determination to press on towards the unknown.

Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the starship *Stardust* through the swirling nebula of Xylos.  Her mission: to retrieve a vital energy source from the distant planet Kepler-186f, a world teeming with both breathtaking beauty and lethal dangers.  Navigating asteroid fields, evading space pirates, and facing the unpredictable quirks of hyperspace travel were all in a day's work.  The vastness of space, both awe-inspiring and terrifying, held the promise of untold discovery, but the journey demanded unwavering skill and nerve.
