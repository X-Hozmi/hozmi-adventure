
The year is 1347.  A biting wind whipped across the muddy track as Thomas, his worn leather jerkin flapping, urged his mule onward.  His quest: to reach the mythical city of Eldoria, whispered to hold the cure for his ailing sister.  Each creaking wagon wheel, each rustle in the nearby forest, held the promise of danger – bandits, wild beasts, and the ever-present threat of starvation.  Yet, the allure of Eldoria, fueled by hope and driven by desperation, pushed him further into the unknown heart of Europe.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova piloted the *Stardust Drifter* through a nebula shimmering with incandescent gas.  Her mission: to locate the lost colony of Xylos, stranded on the far side of the Andromeda galaxy.  Navigating treacherous asteroid fields and dodging the predatory warships of the Kryll Hegemony, Eva's journey was a gamble against insurmountable odds. But the potential discovery of Xylos’s advanced technology, and the hope of rescuing its inhabitants, was a risk worth taking in the boundless expanse of the cosmos.
