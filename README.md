
The year is 1347.  Dust billowed behind Elara's mule as she crested the pass, the wind whipping her cloak around her like a frantic bird.  Ahead lay the sprawling, unknown lands of the Eastern Empire, a tapestry woven with rumors of silk roads, fearsome beasts, and cities glittering with unimaginable wealth.  Each day brought new challenges – bandits lurking in shadowed forests, treacherous river crossings, and the ever-present threat of starvation. Yet, the lure of the unknown, the thirst for discovery, pushed her onward, a lone speck against the vast, indifferent canvas of the medieval world.


Centuries later, Captain Eva Rostova gripped the controls of her starship, the *Stardust Wanderer*,  as it jumped across the Andromeda galaxy.  Navigating the treacherous nebulae and avoiding the gravity wells of undiscovered planets was a constant test of skill and nerve.  Her mission: to locate the mythical Xylos system, rumored to hold the key to faster-than-light travel and untold technological advancements.  The vastness of space, both beautiful and terrifying, pressed in on her, but the promise of galactic exploration fueled her unwavering determination.
